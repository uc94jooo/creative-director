# Inheritance (Skoda · 2008)

## Insight
Cars are passed down through families like heirlooms — sometimes with affection, sometimes with mixed feelings. The emotional relationship to an inherited car is rarely simple, and rarely acknowledged in automotive advertising.

## Mechanic
A comedic film exploring the mixed emotions of inheriting a family car — the obligation, the sentiment, the slight embarrassment — in Skoda's warm-but-wry tonal register.

## Why it worked
Automotive advertising typically shows aspirational new car ownership. The inheritance scenario inverted the aspiration: this is a car you have because of someone else's affection, not your own choice. The human specificity made it memorable in a category of generic aspiration.

## Steal
The most underused angle in category advertising is the awkward, ambivalent human truth about the product's role in real life. Aspiration is crowded; ambivalence is empty.
