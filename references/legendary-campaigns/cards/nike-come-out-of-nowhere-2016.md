# Come Out of Nowhere (Nike · 2016)

## Insight
The greatest athletic stories — culturally, emotionally, and commercially — are about the athlete no one expected. Coming out of nowhere is not just an athletic fact; it is the most resonant form of aspiration because it requires no pedigree, no funding, no institutional support.

## Mechanic
A Rio Olympics film featuring athletes from countries not traditionally associated with their sports — unfancied nations, unexpected competitors, surprising medalists. The "come out of nowhere" framing celebrates the underdog as the truest expression of athletic ambition.

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16): the individual stories of unexpected athletic achievement require time to establish the obstacle before delivering the inversion. Truth telling (P13): the athletes featured are real; their achievements are documented. "Come out of nowhere" is a phrase that belongs to Nike's defiant register without requiring explanation.

## Steal
The underdog story at the Olympics is renewable every four years with entirely new characters. The insight (anyone can come from nowhere) is universal; the stories change with every Games. Build the platform for the insight, not for the execution.
