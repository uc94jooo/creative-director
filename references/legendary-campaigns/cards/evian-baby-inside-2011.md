# Baby Inside (Evian · 2011)

## Insight
The Evian "live young" platform established that youth is internal — a state of mind and body rather than an age. The logical extreme: every adult has a baby inside them, waiting to come out when they drink Evian.

## Mechanic
A spot where adults going about their daily lives catch their reflections — but the reflections show babies performing the same actions. A businessman catches his baby reflection dancing. The premise is played straight, not for laughs. Part of the Evian "live young" ongoing series following Roller Babies.

## Why it worked
P11 (absurd as carrier): the absurdism is gentler here than Roller Babies — more conceptual than spectacular. The conceit (baby reflections) is simple enough to execute without CGI complexity. It extends the Roller Babies brand idea in a more intimate, personal register, turning the campaign from spectacle into insight.

## Steal
After establishing a spectacular campaign, the follow-up can afford to be more intimate. The audience already knows the brand's absurdist mode; the next iteration can demonstrate the idea's depth rather than its scale.
