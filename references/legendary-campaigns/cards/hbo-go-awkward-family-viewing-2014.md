# Awkward Family Viewing (HBO Go · 2014)

## Insight
HBO's explicit content creates genuinely awkward viewing situations when parents, children or coworkers are in the room — a shared cultural embarrassment no one discusses.

## Mechanic
A series of comedy spots dramatizing the specific agony of watching HBO content with the wrong companion: nudity scenes with parents, violence with in-laws, sex scenes with coworkers. Each scenario ends with a pitch for HBO Go — watch on your own device, alone.

## Why it worked
The campaign named a real behavior and turned product utility (personal mobile viewing) into a social escape hatch. Absurdist comedy made the awkwardness shareable rather than shameful.

## Steal
Find the most embarrassing use-case for your product and make it the hero scenario. Naming the awkward truth creates recognition and positions the solution simultaneously.
