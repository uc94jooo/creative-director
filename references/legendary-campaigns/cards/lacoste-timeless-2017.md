# Timeless (Lacoste · 2017)

## Insight
Some products outlast the moments they were born in — they become witnesses to an entire life. The crocodile polo isn't just clothing; it's a recurring character in someone's personal history.

## Mechanic
A long-form film that follows a couple's relationship across decades, with the Lacoste polo shirt as the recurring visual anchor through changing decades, fashions, and life events. The garment's constancy is the implicit product claim.

## Why it worked
The serialization mechanic (P10) applied within a single film: repetition of the same object across time creates cumulative emotional weight. Long-form drama (P16) allows the relationship narrative to build to the point where the polo is genuinely moving rather than merely present.

## Steal
A single object repeated across a long-form timeline does more brand-building work than any product demonstration. The object becomes a character; the brand becomes part of memory rather than marketing.
