# Steeplechase (Weetabix · 2021)

## Insight
Weetabix's "Weetabix with beans" tweet in 2021 became one of the most viral food moments in UK social media history — the brand discovered that deliberately wrong food combinations generate massive organic engagement. The internet loves controlled disgust.

## Mechanic
Building on the beans tweet phenomenon (P05 — cultural hijack of food discourse), Weetabix continued the absurdist "wrong combinations" territory with further content. "Steeplechase" likely extended this absurdist food platform (P11) into new territory, using the brand's established reputation for food-combination provocations to generate further conversation.

## Why it worked
The beans tweet had established Weetabix as the cereal brand that says the quiet part loud about food. P11 (absurdism) and P05 (cultural hijack of food/meal discourse) together gave the brand a genuinely ownable territory: the cereal brand that provocatively misuses its own product. The internet participates organically because the wrong combination format is endlessly generatable.

## Steal
If your brand accidentally discovers a cultural territory (like Weetabix + beans), systematize it immediately. The first viral moment proves the insight; the following executions own the territory.
