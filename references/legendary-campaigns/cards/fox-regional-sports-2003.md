# Regional Sports (FOX Sports · 2003)

## Insight
TODO: Verify year. Regional sports broadcasting occupies an unusual cultural space — hyper-local fandom, specific team loyalties, rituals that outsiders don't understand. FOX Sports could use the specificity of regional sports culture as comedic material.

## Mechanic
An absurdist campaign leaning into the peculiarities of regional sports fandom — likely featuring exaggerated local fan behavior, regional stereotypes, or sports-specific rituals played for comedy.

## Why it worked
Regional sports audiences have strong in-group identity. Absurdist comedy that references their specific cultural context (the regional team, the local stadium ritual, the specific loss) creates belonging through recognition.

## Steal
Hyper-local specificity in humor reads as authenticity to the in-group. National brands that adopt regional specificity earn more trust from that region than generic national campaigns do.
