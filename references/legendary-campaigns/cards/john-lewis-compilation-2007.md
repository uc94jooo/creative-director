# John Lewis Christmas Compilation 2007–2013 (John Lewis · 2007)

## Insight
Christmas advertising typically centres on products and deals; the gift's emotional meaning — what it communicates about the giver's attention and care — is the actual reason people shop at John Lewis.

## Mechanic
A series of annual Christmas films, each telling a short emotional story about the act of giving. The tone is quiet, observational, and structurally built around a reveal — the true meaning of a gift understood only at the end.

## Why it worked
The annual ritual created anticipation; the consistent tonal identity created trust; the emotional reveal each year created sharing. John Lewis occupied Christmas as a brand platform, not a promotional period.

## Steal
Establish a seasonal ritual before trying to execute it. The anticipation the audience brings to an annual campaign amplifies the emotional impact of the work beyond what its individual quality could achieve alone.
