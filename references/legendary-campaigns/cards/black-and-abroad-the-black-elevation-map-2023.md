# The Black Elevation Map (Black & Abroad · 2023)

## Insight

Black travelers face safety concerns and social hostility that other travelers don't. Standard travel tools (Google Maps, TripAdvisor) are built by and for the default traveler, with no information about where Black travelers are welcomed, celebrated, or at risk.

## Mechanic

Black & Abroad built a crowd-sourced interactive map where Black travelers rated destinations, businesses, and experiences by safety, welcome, and Black cultural richness — a parallel travel infrastructure built for and by Black travelers.

## Why it worked

Tech as canvas meets design as idea: the product was genuinely useful, not just symbolic. By building infrastructure that didn't exist, Black & Abroad created something that competitors (general travel brands) could not duplicate without building from scratch with the wrong audience.

## Steal

Find the data infrastructure your audience needs that no existing platform provides because they were not built with your audience in mind. Build it. Own the data. Become the authority for that audience's decisions.
