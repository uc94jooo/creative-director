# Go With the Flaw (Diesel · 2017)

## Insight
The fashion industry obsessively corrects, smooths, and perfects. Diesel's brand position — rebellious, anti-establishment — demands the opposite: celebrate the flaws, the mistakes, the imperfections that make individuals irreplaceable. The flaw is the proof of authenticity.

## Mechanic
A campaign and film celebrating human imperfection — physical, social, behavioural — as expressions of authentic identity rather than failures to be corrected. The tone is celebratory and defiant, not sentimental.

## Why it worked
P03 (behavior inversion): in a category that sells aspiration and correction, Diesel sells the opposite — the flaw as feature. P16 gives the film enough time to make the argument with specificity. The campaign is a direct expression of Diesel's anti-fashion fashion positioning: the brand that built its identity on deliberately wrong choices (the "misspelled" logo, the countercultural casting) has authentic standing to celebrate imperfection.

## Steal
If your brand was built on doing something the category said you shouldn't, that founding act of "wrongness" is your permanent differentiator. Every subsequent campaign should be a new manifestation of the same original refusal to conform.
