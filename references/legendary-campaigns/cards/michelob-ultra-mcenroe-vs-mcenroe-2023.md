# McEnroe vs. McEnroe (Michelob Ultra · 2023)

## Insight

John McEnroe was one of the greatest tennis players of all time — but what would he lose to? The AI-generated version of his peak self. The question of whether peak human ability can be replicated by AI was both technically fascinating and emotionally loaded.

## Mechanic

Long-form film in which 64-year-old John McEnroe played against an AI-generated version of his 1984 peak-performance self — trained on thousands of hours of his historical match footage. Real human vs. AI human. The result was not predetermined.

## Why it worked

Tech as canvas meets long-form drama: the technology (AI coaching model playing on-screen as a younger McEnroe) was the narrative engine, not decoration. The existential question (can AI beat the person it was modeled on?) was genuinely interesting to answer. The result carried real dramatic stakes.

## Steal

When using AI in a campaign, find a question worth answering rather than a feature worth demonstrating. The best AI campaigns are experiments with uncertain outcomes — the technology earns its place only if the audience genuinely doesn't know what will happen.
