# Baby Stroller Test-Ride (Contours · 2023)

## Insight

Parents buy strollers based on specs and reviews — but they never experience what it actually feels like to be the baby inside. The most important user's perspective is the one never consulted.

## Mechanic

Contours built full-size baby strollers that adults could ride in — and let expectant parents test-ride them to experience stroller quality from the baby's perspective. The role-reversal made product quality tangible in a completely novel way.

## Why it worked

Truth telling meets user as co-author: the insight (the baby's experience is the purchase decision's actual subject) was revealed through participation rather than stated. Adults in baby strollers was inherently shareable and press-worthy.

## Steal

Find the primary user of your product who is not the purchaser. Design an experience that makes the purchaser take that user's perspective. The perspective-shift becomes the product demo.
