# The Last Barf Bag (Dramamine · 2023)

## Insight

Airlines have been removing barf bags from seat pockets — but motion sickness hasn't gone away, leaving travelers in a worse position than before. The disappearing barf bag is a culturally recognized, relatable anxiety.

## Mechanic

Dramamine collected the "last barf bags" from airlines that were eliminating them, auctioned them as collectibles, and turned the artifact of motion sickness into a cultural moment — positioning Dramamine as the solution to a problem everyone recognizes but no one talks about.

## Why it worked

Absurdism meets user participation: the barf bag as collectible object inverted its disgusting utility into a conversation starter. By making a taboo subject into an auctionable artifact, Dramamine earned press, social sharing, and genuine product relevance.

## Steal

Find the most embarrassing product in your category. Make that embarrassment the hero by treating it with unexpected seriousness or desirability. Taboo subjects earn attention precisely because no one else is touching them.
