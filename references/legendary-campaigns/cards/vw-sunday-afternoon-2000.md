## Mechanic
A quiet, observational film set on a lazy Sunday afternoon — likely a young couple or individual experiencing a moment of freedom or escape, scored to a melancholic indie/folk track. The VW appears as companion to the mood rather than hero of the story.

## Why it worked
VW's late-1990s/early-2000s campaign era (Arnold Worldwide) pioneered the music-as-soul approach in automotive advertising. Rather than feature specs, they made the car feel like a feeling. "Sunday Afternoon" exemplifies the format: the car earns belonging through mood fit.

## Steal
When a product cannot differentiate on specs, make it the emotional enabler of a lifestyle moment. Music selection and pace do more persuasive work than features.
