# Burger Glitch (Burger King · 2023)

## Insight
Gaming audiences are fluent in glitches — unintended visual or mechanical errors that break the game world's rules. Glitches are simultaneously frustrating and endearing; communities celebrate and share them. Burger King's bright, hyperreal colour palette looks exactly like a mobile game.

## Mechanic
Burger King created social content that mimicked the visual style of a gaming glitch — the brand's food and design appeared to "break" the screen, overflow menus, and malfunction in ways that looked native to mobile gaming interfaces. Engagement required identifying the "glitch."

## Why it worked
Format as idea (P01): the glitch format is the concept — it cannot be executed in any other medium without losing meaning. Absurdism (P11): the internal logic of the glitch world is followed consistently. The execution is cheap and algorithmically native to the feeds it appeared in.

## Steal
Identify the visual languages native to your audience's primary screen time. Speak in those languages — not about them. Being mistaken for organic content is not a failure; it's the highest form of attention efficiency.
