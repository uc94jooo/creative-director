# The DNA Journey (Momondo · 2016)

## Insight

Nationalist pride and prejudice are built on the fiction of ethnic purity. DNA testing reveals that no one is only from where they think they are — we are all genetically mixed, and travel reveals the same truth.

## Mechanic

Social experiment: people with strong national or ethnic identities take DNA tests on camera. Their results — unexpected genetic heritage from countries they'd stereotyped or opposed — are revealed in real-time emotional reaction. Momondo positions itself as the brand of open exploration.

## Why it worked

Social experiment meets long-form drama: the DNA results functioned as undeniable evidence that the audience's prejudices couldn't argue with. The emotional reactions were genuine, unscripted, and structurally guaranteed — anyone's DNA disproves their nationalism. The brand as facilitator of discovery rather than claimant.

## Steal

Find a scientific truth that directly contradicts your audience's most confidently held belief. Design an experiment that reveals it experientially rather than arguing against it. Evidence beats argument every time.
