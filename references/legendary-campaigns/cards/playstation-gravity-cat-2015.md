# Gravity Cat (PlayStation · 2015)

## Insight

Gaming allows you to experience physics and realities that don't exist in the real world. PlayStation is the machine that makes the impossible feel real.

## Mechanic

Absurdist film and integrated campaign around a giant cat that defies gravity — the impossible physics of the game world made literal as a live-action spectacle. Cat scale and gravity inversion created a surreal visual world matching the game's reality-bending mechanics.

## Why it worked

Absurdism meets tech as canvas: the visual impossibility of the campaign content mirrored the visual impossibility of the game world it promoted. The absurd was not decoration — it was the brief being executed.

## Steal

When promoting a game or experience that defies real-world physics, make the campaign's visuals equally physics-defying. The medium should demonstrate what the product feels like.
