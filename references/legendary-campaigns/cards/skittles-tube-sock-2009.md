# Tube Sock (Skittles · 2009)

## Insight
Everyday mundane objects — a tube sock — become strange when Skittles logic is applied. The brand's absurdism operates by taking the most ordinary starting point and following it to an impossible conclusion.

## Mechanic
A short absurdist film featuring a tube sock in a Skittles scenario, following the brand's "Touch the Rainbow" logic.

## Why it worked
Mundane starting points (a tube sock, a man, a house) make the absurdist destination more surprising by contrast. The gap between ordinary premise and impossible conclusion is where Skittles' humor lives.

## Steal
The more mundane the setup, the more effective the absurdist punchline. Don't start strange — start familiar and go strange.
