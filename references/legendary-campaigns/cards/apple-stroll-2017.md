# Stroll (Apple · 2017)

## Insight

Music through quality audio changes the experience of simply moving through space — a walk becomes something else entirely when the soundtrack is right.

## Mechanic

Short film following a person on a walk through a city, with Apple audio (AirPods/Apple Music) transforming the mundane journey into something cinematic — the world syncing with the music.

## Why it worked

Part of Apple's craft-first film series: by showing the interior experience of quality audio through exterior storytelling, the films made intangible audio quality feel visible.

## Steal

Take the most ordinary version of your product use case. Render the internal experience as an external spectacle. The contrast between mundane trigger and extraordinary feeling is the idea.
