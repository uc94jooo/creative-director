# Most Dangerous Band in the World (FedEx · 2016)

## Insight
FedEx delivers dangerous goods — hazardous materials, explosives, chemicals — under strict regulations. The absurdist question: what would the most dangerous musical instrument delivery look like?

## Mechanic
A comedic long-form film exploring what happens when a band composed of the world's most dangerous musical instruments — presumably explosive, radioactive, or otherwise hazardous band equipment — needs to be shipped. FedEx is the only company capable of handling the shipment.

## Why it worked
Absurdism (P11) used to dramatise a genuine product attribute (FedEx's capacity to handle hazardous and complex shipments) through an escalating ridiculous scenario. The long-form structure (P16) allows the absurd premise to compound through multiple surreal complications before resolving on the FedEx solution.

## Steal
Dangerous or complex product attributes can be dramatised through the most extreme possible absurdist scenario requiring that capability. The scenario doesn't need to be realistic — it needs to be funny enough that the underlying capability registers.
