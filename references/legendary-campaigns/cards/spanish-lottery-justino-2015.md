# Justino (Spanish Christmas Lottery · 2015)

## Insight
The Spanish Christmas Lottery is a national ritual — families, coworkers, entire villages buy tickets together. The emotional truth is not the prize; it's the shared anticipation and what it means to win alongside the people you love.

## Mechanic
Justino is a lonely night-shift worker at a mannequin factory who secretly creates playful scenes with the mannequins to delight his day-shift colleagues. When he wins the lottery, he shares his ticket with them — strangers made family by proximity and a gesture of generosity.

## Why it worked
The film works on the P16 long-form drama mechanic: a character with a clear desire (connection), obstacles (the night shift, invisibility), and a resolution that reframes the lottery from individual luck to collective joy. The mannequin world is visually distinctive. Justino is genuinely moving without sentiment.

## Steal
When you have a product associated with luck or chance, reframe it around generosity and community. The prize is not the point; what you do with it is.
