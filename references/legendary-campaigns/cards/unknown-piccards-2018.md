# The Piccards (unknown · 2018)

## Insight
TODO: Verify brand. A character-driven long-form film using a family narrative to explore how a technology or telecom product changes the texture of everyday life.

## Mechanic
Long-form character study following a family — possibly named the Piccards — navigating modern life with the product integrated into their story. The family drama format is the vehicle for product demonstration.

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16): the family unit is the most universal narrative container for technology communication. Family stories can compress years of change into minutes while maintaining emotional credibility. Character-led demonstration > feature-led demonstration.

## Steal
Technology adoption is always a family story — one member is the early adopter, one is resistant, children adapt fastest. The drama of that dynamic is more compelling than any spec sheet.
