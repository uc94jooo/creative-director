# Chasing Horizons (Citizen · 2018)

## Insight
A watch brand lives at the intersection of time and aspiration. The horizon is the metaphor that connects both: something always ahead, always pursued, never quite reached — which is the emotional territory of both time and ambition.

## Mechanic
A film following individuals across landscapes — physical and metaphorical — as they pursue their version of the horizon. Cinematic and aspirational, with Citizen's light-powered Eco-Drive technology as the enabling product truth (powered by light, like the horizon itself).

## Why it worked
P16 (long-form drama) in the luxury watch tradition of building emotional territory rather than demonstrating product features. The horizon metaphor is broad enough to be universal while giving the film a consistent visual logic. The product's actual attribute — light-powered — provides a grounding link to the metaphor that prevents the campaign from feeling purely decorative.

## Steal
When your product has a functional attribute that can be poetically reframed as a metaphor, let the metaphor carry the film and use the attribute as the anchor. The metaphor earns emotion; the attribute earns credibility.
