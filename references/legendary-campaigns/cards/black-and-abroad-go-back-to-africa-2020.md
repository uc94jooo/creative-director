# Go Back to Africa (Black & Abroad · 2020)

## Insight

"Go back to Africa" is a racist slur used against Black people in the West — but Black & Abroad is a Black travel company specializing in African travel. The slur is actually an invitation.

## Mechanic

Black & Abroad used AI to monitor racist "Go back to Africa" tweets, then replied to each with stunning photography and video of the exact African destination that tweet inadvertently suggested. The racist slur became a travel recommendation engine.

## Why it worked

Behavior inversion meets user participation: the most hostile phrase directed at Black people was reclaimed as the brand's own tagline by reversing its intent. The AI-powered response system scaled the reclamation while the photography made Africa desirable. Racists became unwilling brand distributors.

## Steal

Find the most hostile thing said about your audience. Ask what it would mean if you took it literally and positively. Reclamation at scale — especially when automated — turns opponents into amplifiers.
