# Cousins (HBO Go · 2014)

## Insight
Watching HBO content with relatives you barely know creates a specific kind of sustained social agony — particularly during scenes involving sex, violence, or moral complexity.

## Mechanic
A short film in the "Awkward Family Viewing" series depicting the excruciating experience of watching HBO content with estranged or unfamiliar cousins — leading to the pitch for HBO Go as a private viewing solution.

## Why it worked
The comedy works because the situation is hyper-specific (cousins — not parents, not strangers, not close friends) which makes it instantly recognizable to a wide audience. Specificity in social comedy beats generality.

## Steal
In social-situation comedy, the more precisely you name the awkward relationship (not "family" but "cousins who you only see at Christmas"), the more people feel accurately described.
