# Unlimited Stadium (Nike · 2016)

## Insight
Runners in Manila are motivated by competition but lack the infrastructure and community to experience it regularly — running alone is a discipline problem.

## Mechanic
An LED track in the shape of a running shoe, 200 metres long, where runners race against a digital avatar of their own best time. The personal best was visualized as a competitor running alongside them in real time.

## Why it worked
The technology made the runner's own progress visible as a tangible opponent. Self-competition — normally abstract — became embodied and spatial. The product (running shoes, Nike+) and the experience were inseparable.

## Steal
Turn a runner's invisible internal goal (personal best) into a physical competitor that can be defeated. When abstract motivation becomes concrete opposition, the behaviour becomes dramatically more engaging.
