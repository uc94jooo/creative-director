# The Refugee Edition (Libération · 2015)

## Insight
News coverage of the refugee crisis showed statistics and tragedy from a distance. What would it mean for refugees to have their own voice in a mainstream publication — not as subjects of coverage but as authors?

## Mechanic
French newspaper Libération handed over an entire edition to refugees — they wrote articles, took photographs, and told their own stories. The newspaper's design language remained intact; only the bylines changed. The edition was distributed normally.

## Why it worked
The inversion of subject/author is the truth-telling mechanism. Design as idea (P17) makes the argument visible: the same newspaper, the same design, but different voices — demonstrating that the gap between refugee and journalist is human circumstance, not inherent difference.

## Steal
Find the one editorial or production decision that, if changed, would make your brand's stated values structurally visible rather than rhetorically stated. Then make that decision publicly.
