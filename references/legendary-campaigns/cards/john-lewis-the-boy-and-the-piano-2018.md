# The Boy & the Piano (John Lewis & Partners · 2018)

## Insight
A gift at the right moment can unlock the trajectory of an entire life — John Lewis as the place where those formative gifts are found.

## Mechanic
A film tracing Elton John's life in reverse from his global fame back to a young boy receiving a piano as a Christmas gift from his grandmother — that single present the origin point of everything. Elton John narrates and performs.

## Why it worked
Reverse chronology from peak achievement to childhood gift makes the product's emotional argument concrete: this is what a gift can do. Celebrity casting elevated to genuine personal biography rather than endorsement. The grandmother becomes the hero.

## Steal
Reverse-trace a famous outcome to the gift or moment that triggered it. The argument becomes: ordinary acts of giving have extraordinary long-term consequences. Celebrity biography as product proof.
