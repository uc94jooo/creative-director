# Corazon: Give Your Heart (Montefiore Health System · 2018)

## Insight

Heart disease is the #1 killer, yet people donate organs far less than they could. The emotional distance from death makes organ donation feel abstract — until it becomes personal.

## Mechanic

Long-form film following a heart transplant patient and the donor's family — told through the literal and metaphorical journey of a heart, humanizing the medical procedure into a story of love persisting beyond death.

## Why it worked

Long-form drama meets truth telling: healthcare advertising typically avoids the weight of death. By leaning into grief and gift simultaneously, the film earned genuine emotional resonance rather than manufactured uplift.

## Steal

In a category that systematically avoids its most emotionally charged truths, the brand that tells the real story earns disproportionate trust and attention.
