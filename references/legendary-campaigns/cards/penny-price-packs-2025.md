# Price Packs (PENNY · 2025)

## Insight
Discount retail faces a communications paradox: communicate low prices without communicating low quality. Design-led communication (P17) can signal quality while the mechanic communicates value.

## Mechanic
An integrated campaign combining participatory mechanics (P09) with design-led communication (P17) — using the price packs themselves as the medium. The packaging design carries the campaign message, making the product's value proposition a design statement.

## Why it worked
Retail advertising commonly shouts prices; PENNY used design to whisper them. When the pack becomes the communication, the product is its own media channel. P09 adds participatory momentum: customers engaging with or sharing the packs.

## Steal
Turn your lowest-cost packaging into your highest-quality brand communication. The constraint of affordability becomes the canvas for design intelligence.
