# 23 vs. 39 (Gatorade · 2009)

## Insight
Michael Jordan at 23 was the greatest athlete alive. Michael Jordan at 39, past retirement, playing again — what would happen? The insight is that the legend's performance at peak is incomparable to any human rival, but what about the legend against himself across time? That is the only match-up worth watching.

## Mechanic
A film pitting a digitally recreated 23-year-old Michael Jordan against the 39-year-old Michael Jordan in a one-on-one game. Both versions animated and stylized. The competition is between the greatest player alive and the greatest version of himself — implying that Gatorade fuels athletes across decades, not just in their prime.

## Why it worked
P16 (long-form drama): the emotional weight comes from the mythological confrontation — prime Jordan vs. older Jordan. P05 (cultural hijack): Jordan returning from retirement was a cultural event; the campaign inserts itself into that narrative by asking the question the entire sports world was already asking. The film extends the brand's long association with Jordan without relying on product placement.

## Steal
When your brand has a long-standing relationship with a cultural legend, create a confrontation between versions of that legend. It is more interesting than a simple tribute and more memorable than a celebrity endorsement.
