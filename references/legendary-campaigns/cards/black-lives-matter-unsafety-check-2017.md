# Unsafety Check (Black Lives Matter · 2017)

## Insight

Facebook's "Safety Check" feature — designed to reassure loved ones after disasters — was never triggered for Black Americans killed by police, exposing the systemic blind spot in whose safety is institutionally marked.

## Mechanic

Black Lives Matter inverted Facebook's Safety Check mechanic: a social tool that let Black Americans "check in as unsafe" in response to police violence incidents, reframing the platform's own language against the absence of protection.

## Why it worked

Truth telling meets brand-as-activist: the campaign used an existing platform mechanic to expose its own inadequacy. The inversion was both conceptually precise and emotionally devastating — the gap between "safe" and "unsafe" was the message.

## Steal

Find a platform mechanic that was designed to include everyone but functionally excludes your audience. Use the mechanic against itself.
