# Beds (IKEA · 2018)

## Insight
The bed is the most intimate piece of furniture in any home — the site of sleep, sex, arguments, illness, and comfort. IKEA sells beds; the brand can approach that intimacy with either sentimentality or absurdist self-awareness.

## Mechanic
A short film using IKEA beds as the comic prop — exploring the various human situations that happen in, around, or because of a bed, with IKEA's characteristic tone of gentle absurdism.

## Why it worked
P11 (absurd as carrier) applied to domestic intimacy — IKEA is at home in the most literal space, and the absurdist approach to that space is more charming than the sentimental approach that competitor furniture brands take. The execution is lightweight but consistent with brand tone.

## Steal
The most intimate product categories are the ones most prone to sentimentality — which means absurdism is the bigger differentiator in those categories, not in categories that are already comic.
