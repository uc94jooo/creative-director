# Follow the Arches: Year 1 (McDonald's · 2019)

## Insight

The Follow the Arches design language — logo fragment as directional tool — can extend into a serialized system, with each year adding new applications of the same foundational visual idea.

## Mechanic

Year 1 extension of the Follow the Arches platform: new OOH applications using fragmented arches as navigation and wayfinding tools, building the system across more markets and contexts.

## Why it worked

Serialization of a design idea: once the visual language was proven in Year 0, the system could expand. Each new execution reinforced the brand confidence argument without repeating it.

## Steal

A strong design idea should have years of executions available, not just one campaign burst. Build the system first; the executions follow.
