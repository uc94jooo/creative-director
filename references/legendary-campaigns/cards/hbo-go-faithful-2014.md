# Faithful (HBO Go · 2014)

## Insight
Watching explicit or morally complex HBO content with someone who holds different values — a religious family member, a conservative partner — creates an acute shared discomfort that neither party can address.

## Mechanic
A short film in the "Awkward Family Viewing" series depicting the specific agony of watching HBO content alongside someone whose beliefs make the content doubly awkward. The resolution: HBO Go, so you never have to share the screen again.

## Why it worked
The scenario targets a specific value-gap discomfort that exists in many real households. By naming a specific audience segment's specific problem (differing values in a shared viewing situation), the product utility becomes immediately legible.

## Steal
Identify the most culturally specific version of your user's problem — not "uncomfortable with others" but "uncomfortable with this specific type of other person for this specific reason." Maximum specificity in problem description makes the solution feel custom-built.
