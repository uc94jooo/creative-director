# Sweat it Out (Levi's · 2018)

## Insight

TODO: Year inferred; verify against campaign records.

Effort and physical work are where authentic character is built — denim is the fabric of people who do things with their hands and bodies, not just their screens.

## Mechanic

Film emphasizing physical effort, craft, and the satisfaction of working hard — featuring real people in real labor, positioning Levi's as the brand of people who sweat and build.

## Why it worked

Craft as message meets long-form drama: physical authenticity in fashion advertising is rare. Sweat, labor, and effort are category-disruptive when most denim advertising uses aspirational leisure contexts.

## Steal

Show the effort your product enables or endures, rather than the leisure it enables. Physical truth reads as authenticity in a category full of polished aspiration.
