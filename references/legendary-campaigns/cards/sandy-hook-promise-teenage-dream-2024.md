# Teenage Dream (Sandy Hook Promise · 2024)

## Insight
Teenagers documenting their adolescence on phones — parties, graduations, sleepovers — and teenagers documenting active shooter incidents on the same phones, with the same trembling hands, in the same format. Both exist. Most people have seen only one.

## Mechanic
A film structured as a nostalgic teen-memory montage set to Katy Perry's "Teenage Dream" — the quintessential track of youthful optimism. As the montage progresses, the footage shifts: the joyful memories and the shooter-incident recordings become intercut, then indistinguishable. The song continues without pause.

## Why it worked
The contrast between soundtrack (euphoric) and content (terror) creates cognitive dissonance that bypasses the audience's accustomed defences to school shooting content. Behaviour inversion (P03): the format of joyful teen nostalgia is the shell for an unbearable reality. Truth-telling (P13): the footage style is consistent with what exists — these recordings are real in form even if not in specific content.

## Steal
When an audience has been numbed to the direct presentation of a horror, approach it through the aesthetic language of its opposite. Euphoric music over graphic content creates a dissonance the brain cannot smoothly process — and that discomfort carries the message.
