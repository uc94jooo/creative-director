# The Greatest (Apple · 2023)

## Insight

People with disabilities are not "despite" who they are — they are exactly who they are. Apple's accessibility features are not special accommodations; they are how Apple designs for everyone.

## Mechanic

Film showing disabled people doing extraordinary things using iPhone accessibility features — the absurdist angle is that what appears superhuman (seeing without eyes, communicating without voice, navigating without touch) is simply Apple's standard functionality in action.

## Why it worked

Absurdism meets long-form drama: by framing standard accessibility features as "the greatest" human capabilities, the film inverted the usual narrative of disability (limitation → accommodation) into one of capability → freedom. No special treatment, no inspiration porn — just competence.

## Steal

Find the user population your product serves that your category systematically ignores. Make their use case the hero, not the edge case. If the feature is genuinely good, it doesn't need to be framed as a gesture of inclusion.
