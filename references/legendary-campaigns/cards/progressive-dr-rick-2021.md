# Dr. Rick (Progressive · 2021)

## Insight
People become their parents. It's inevitable, universal, and secretly terrifying — especially when it comes to homeowner habits. Insurance exists at exactly the life stage when this transformation happens.

## Mechanic
Dr. Rick, a "parentamorphosis" specialist, helps new homeowners avoid turning into their parents through absurd therapeutic interventions. The recurring character runs across a long campaign of spots treating the joke seriously.

## Why it worked
The serialised character creates appointment viewing in a category (insurance) that generates zero organic interest. The insight (becoming your parents) is universally recognised without being mean. The absurdist therapeutic framing keeps the execution fresh across many executions.

## Steal
Find the universal human anxiety that coincides exactly with your product's moment of purchase. Build a recurring character around it. The ritual of recognising yourself in the joke IS the brand relationship.
