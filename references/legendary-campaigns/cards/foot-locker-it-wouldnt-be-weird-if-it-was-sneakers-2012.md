# It Wouldn't Be Weird If It Was Sneakers (Foot Locker · 2012)

## Insight
Sneakerhead culture involves behaviours that would be considered bizarre if applied to any other object category — queuing overnight, spending rent money, keeping boxes pristine. The campaign's insight: the obsession is real and the audience knows it.

## Mechanic
Print/OOH showing recognisably weird collector behaviour (hoarding, obsessing, prioritising) but with the copy: "It wouldn't be weird if it was sneakers." The design was the punchline — clean, direct, no illustration of the absurdity, only the declaration.

## Why it worked
Insider advertising works when the audience recognises themselves and feels celebrated rather than mocked. The self-awareness was the compliment. Sneakerheads already knew they were weird — someone finally said it and took their side.

## Steal
When your audience has a behaviour they're slightly ashamed of but secretly proud of, naming it with affection beats celebrating it with reverence. The brand that laughs with you is more loved than the brand that worships you.
