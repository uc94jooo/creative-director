# This is How We Work Now (Upwork · 2021)

## Insight

The pandemic permanently changed work — remote and freelance is not a compromise, it's the new normal. Old corporate structures feel like corpses insisting they're still alive.

## Mechanic

Musical spot featuring a literal animated corpse singing about the "old way of working" being dead — absurdist imagery celebrating the shift to freelance and distributed work as progress, not disruption.

## Why it worked

Absurdist drama with precise strategic clarity: the corpse metaphor made an abstract category shift (remote work becoming mainstream) viscerally concrete. Upwork positioned itself as the brand of the new world, not a tool for a transition.

## Steal

When a category is undergoing irreversible change, be the first brand to declare the old order dead — with a visual that makes the death undeniable rather than merely stated.
