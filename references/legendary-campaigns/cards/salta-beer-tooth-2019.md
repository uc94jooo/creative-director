# Beer Tooth (Salta · 2019)

## Insight
Opening a beer bottle with your teeth is a universal bar ritual — a casual display of confidence and camaraderie. The bottle cap is the prop; your teeth are the tool.

## Mechanic
Salta created OOH posters designed to actually open beer bottles — the poster surface had a bottle-cap opener integrated into its design at exactly the right height. The ad becomes the utility.

## Why it worked
Design as idea: the poster is a beer opener. The integration of function into format is seamless and relevant — a beer brand that helps you open beers when you have one in hand. The execution is executed simply and cheaply.

## Steal
Find one practical moment in your audience's relationship with your product category. Design your communication to solve that moment rather than describe it.
