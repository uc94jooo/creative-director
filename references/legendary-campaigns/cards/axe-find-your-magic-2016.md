# Find Your Magic (Axe · 2016)

## Insight
Axe had spent decades selling the fantasy of hyper-masculine seduction; by 2016 that fantasy was culturally exhausted and damaging the brand's relevance with younger men who rejected rigid masculinity.

## Mechanic
A film celebrating men's diversity: a man in heels, a man with a belly, a man in a wheelchair — each owning their identity without apology. Copy: "Forget 6-pack abs, find your magic." Complete tonal inversion of the Axe brand code.

## Why it worked
The brand's oldest convention — the irresistible masculine ideal — was the enemy it needed to defeat first. By voicing what its audience already believed, Axe avoided seeming like a brand lecture and instead felt like validation.

## Steal
When a category's core fantasy has curdled into a cliché your own audience rejects, the biggest creative move is to be the brand that names the problem — even if that problem is your own past communication.
