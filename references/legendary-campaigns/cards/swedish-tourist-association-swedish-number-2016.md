# The Swedish Number (Swedish Tourist Association · 2016)

## Insight
Tourism advertising sells destinations through imagery and aspirational copy. Sweden's real competitive advantage was something money can't buy: the candor of ordinary Swedes. Instead of describing Sweden, you could talk to it.

## Mechanic
Sweden published a single phone number — +46 771 793 336 — that connected any caller in the world to a random Swedish volunteer. No script, no filter. You could ask anything. Over 197 countries called; 32,000+ Swedes signed up as ambassadors. Each conversation was its own PR story.

## Why it worked
User-as-co-author (P09): every volunteer became a live ambassador, every call a piece of earned media. Tech-as-canvas (P18): the telephone infrastructure became the campaign. The mechanic was intrinsically newsworthy — "country publishes national phone number" is a headline that writes itself. Authenticity was guaranteed because it was literally unscripted.

## Steal
When your product is an experience, give the audience access to the experience itself rather than a representation of it. A hotline to a real person beats a glossy film every time. The constraint (volunteers, unknown callers) becomes the proof of brand confidence.
