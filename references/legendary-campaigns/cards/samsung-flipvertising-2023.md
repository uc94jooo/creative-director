# Flipvertising (Samsung · 2023)

## Insight
iPhone users are algorithmically walled off from Samsung ads — the targeting system is the barrier. What if teaching users to hack the algorithm was itself the campaign?

## Mechanic
Samsung explained publicly how iPhone owners could manipulate their own ad targeting to receive Galaxy Z Flip ads, then enter a sweepstake to win the phone. Retargeting was turned into a participation game.

## Why it worked
The audience became the distribution channel. Algorithm gaming as brand engagement created earned media from the mechanic itself — and put iPhone users through a journey that ended with Samsung content they chose to receive.

## Steal
Make the platform's algorithm or system a visible part of the game the audience plays. When users hack infrastructure on your behalf, the participation signals intent more powerfully than passive ad exposure.
