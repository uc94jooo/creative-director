# Moldy Whopper (Burger King · 2020)

## Insight
Food that never rots is not food — it is a chemical product engineered to simulate food. Every day a burger doesn't mould is evidence of the preservatives inside it. Burger King's decision to remove artificial preservatives produces a Whopper that rots naturally. That's the proof.

## Mechanic
Burger King filmed a Whopper decomposing over 34 days — beautiful time-lapse photography of progressive mould colonisation. The campaign ran as film, OOH, and print. No appetite appeal. No product shot at its best. Only the evidence of natural ingredients doing what natural ingredients do.

## Why it worked
Behaviour inversion (P03): showing a product at its worst to prove a quality claim inverts every QSR convention. Design as idea (P17): the rotting Whopper photograph is impossible to look away from — the repulsion IS the memorability. The message requires no copy: a rotting burger proves no artificial preservatives better than any ingredient list.

## Steal
Find the most counterintuitive way to show your product's most important quality claim. If the claim is "natural," the proof might be showing nature doing what nature does — including the parts that make audiences uncomfortable.
