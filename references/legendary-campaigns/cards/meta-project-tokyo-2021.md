# Project Tokyo (Meta · 2021)

## Insight
The Tokyo Olympics were held without spectators due to COVID-19. Athletes competed in silence. Families who trained alongside their athletes for years could not witness their defining moments. Facebook/Meta could be the connection point across that distance.

## Mechanic
A film or campaign built around athletes at the Tokyo Olympics and the loved ones who could not attend — showing how the platform sustained the emotional connection across quarantine and travel restrictions. The pandemic context made the theme of separation and reunion viscerally resonant.

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16): the emotional stakes of the Olympics without spectators required extended treatment. The timing was inherently newsworthy — the empty Olympic stadium was the image of 2021. Connection across distance is Facebook's core claim, and the context made that claim necessary rather than aspirational.

## Steal
Look for moments in the real world where your product's core benefit is desperately needed. Don't create the context — find it. The COVID Olympics was a gift to any brand whose product is human connection.
