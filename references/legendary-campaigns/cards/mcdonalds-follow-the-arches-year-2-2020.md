# Follow the Arches: Year 2 (McDonald's · 2020)

## Insight

The Follow the Arches visual language continues to expand — demonstrating that a strong design idea is a platform, not a campaign, compounding brand recognition over time.

## Mechanic

Second year of the Follow the Arches design system: further OOH applications with fragmented arches adapted to new contexts, locations, and navigation scenarios, deepening the visual language.

## Why it worked

Serialization and ritual: returning to the same design system annually built audience familiarity and anticipation. Each year's execution reinforced the brand confidence that animated Year 0.

## Steal

The best design ideas don't end after one campaign — they become the brand's visual vocabulary. Invest in systems that compound rather than reset.
