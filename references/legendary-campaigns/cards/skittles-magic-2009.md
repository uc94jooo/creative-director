# Magic (Skittles · 2009)

## Insight
TODO: Verify exact year. Part of Skittles' sustained absurdist platform — one of a series of executions exploring transformation, surreal humor, and candy's illogical relationship with reality.

## Mechanic
A Skittles spot using magic or transformation as the absurdist premise — likely featuring an impossible transformation triggered by Skittles, played with complete deadpan sincerity and no explanation offered.

## Why it worked
Skittles was one of the few candy brands to build a consistent creative language out of pure absurdism across multiple years. The "magic" theme connects to the brand's candy-as-transformative-force territory that made spots like "Touch the Rainbow" culturally memorable.

## Steal
Transformation is a universally legible story structure. Absurdist brands can use transformation mechanics (A becomes B because of product) to create memorable visual moments that work without any rational product claim.
