# Straight Outta Somewhere (Beats by Dre · 2015)

## Insight
The release of the N.W.A biopic "Straight Outta Compton" unlocked a cultural moment around pride in hometown identity — every community has a place they're "straight outta."

## Mechanic
Beats created a meme generator tied to the film's release, letting anyone make a "Straight Outta [Hometown]" image in the iconic NWA logo format. Hundreds of millions of shares, including celebrities, athletes, and entire communities.

## Why it worked
The tool tapped pre-existing cultural pride at a moment of peak cultural relevance. The barrier to participation was minimal; the output was shareable; the brand was associated with authentic cultural heritage without needing to claim ownership.

## Steal
When a cultural moment creates a participation template, build the simplest possible tool that lets everyone insert their personal truth. The brand's job is to design the empty frame, not fill it.
