# Money (Barclays · 2014)

## Insight
Money is the category Barclays operates in, but money is rarely talked about honestly — it carries anxiety, aspiration, and meaning beyond its face value. A campaign that acknowledges what money actually represents in people's lives differs from one that simply promotes financial products.

## Mechanic
A long-form film (P16) exploring the emotional and human dimensions of money through craft storytelling (P08). The exact execution details require verification from the source URL.

## Why it worked
Finance advertising habitually avoids the emotional reality of money. P16 and P08 together allow a banking brand to address the gap between what money means and what financial institutions typically say about it.

## Steal
In a category defined by rationality, the brand that speaks to the emotional reality of the product earns disproportionate trust. Craft signals that the topic deserves serious treatment.
