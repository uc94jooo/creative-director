# Climate Doctor's Certificate (School Strike 4 Climate · 2024)

## Insight
Students skipping school for climate strikes face disciplinary consequences — the system punishes the behaviour the crisis demands.

## Mechanic
Doctors (GPs) issued official medical-style certificates excusing students from school for climate strike participation, inserting the cause into a legitimate bureaucratic process that schools could not easily ignore.

## Why it worked
The campaign weaponized an official, trusted institution — the medical certificate — against the authority trying to suppress protest. It created structural legitimacy for civil disobedience.

## Steal
Embed communication inside a mandatory or trusted official process. The authority of the format (medical, legal, governmental) transfers to the message automatically.
