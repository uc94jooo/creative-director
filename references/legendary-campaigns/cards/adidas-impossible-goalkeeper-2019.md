# Impossible Goalkeeper (Adidas · 2019)

## Insight
Sports equipment advertising claims to help you perform better — but the claims are abstract. An experiential mechanic that lets people test against an "impossible" goalkeeper makes the performance claim physical and participatory.

## Mechanic
An experiential activation (P09) featuring an "impossible goalkeeper" — likely a robotic or algorithmically enhanced opponent — that participants attempt to beat. The absurdist element (P11) is in the "impossible" framing. Adidas equipment or technology helps level the playing field.

## Why it worked
Product-as-proof through participation: people experience the brand's performance claim rather than watching it. The "impossible" framing creates a challenge narrative that generates social sharing through personal achievement.

## Steal
Build the product demonstration into a participatory challenge. The challenge creates stakes; the brand helps you meet them. Experience beats assertion every time.
