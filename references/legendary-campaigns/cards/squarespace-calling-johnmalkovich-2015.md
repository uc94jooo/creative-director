# Calling JohnMalkovich.com (Squarespace · 2015)

## Insight

Anyone can build a website — including John Malkovich, who wants to design a fashion line. Squarespace's product democratizes presence that previously required infrastructure.

## Mechanic

Super Bowl spot: John Malkovich, furious that someone else owns JohnMalkovich.com, calls the squatter and pleads for his domain. The absurdity of a famous person not owning their own name online dramatized exactly what Squarespace solves.

## Why it worked

Absurd as carrier meets tech as canvas: the comic scenario had an exact logical connection to the product's real function (own your online presence). The celebrity's predicament was the product demo — without a demo.

## Steal

Cast someone famous in the exact situation your product solves. The irony of their status (famous person lacks digital presence) does the brief's work without stating the benefit.
