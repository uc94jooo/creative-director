# Exclusive the Rainbow (Skittles · 2019)

## Insight
Super Bowl advertising is the most expensive, most-watched commercial real estate on earth. What if a brand made an ad only one person could see?

## Mechanic
For Super Bowl 2019, Skittles created "the most exclusive ad ever" — a live commercial performed only for a single viewer, Marcus Crandall, in his home. Everyone else watched a live stream of his reaction rather than the ad itself. The scarcity is the spectacle.

## Why it worked
Anti-advertising as advertising. By refusing to show the ad, Skittles generated more coverage than the ad itself would have earned. The meta-commentary on Super Bowl advertising is itself the brand's irreverent personality made structural.

## Steal
When you can't out-spend the room, out-weird it. The absence of the expected thing (the ad) becomes the story. Scarcity of access can be manufactured by design.
