# Green Dawn (Ubisoft · 2020)

## Insight
When real-world events start to resemble video game plots, the line between fiction and reality blurs — and a gaming brand can exploit that blur for maximum attention.

## Mechanic
Ubisoft released a fake government PSA about an alien invasion — the type of absurdist premise central to their Far Cry: New Dawn game — as if it were a real public safety announcement, hijacking the format of official crisis communications.

## Why it worked
The stunt works on two levels: gamers in on the joke, non-gamers confused enough to share. The format inversion (official PSA for absurd content) is the mechanic. Speed of the reveal maintains audience trust.

## Steal
Borrow the most formal, trust-laden format in your target audience's world and fill it with something absurd. The gap between form and content is where attention lives.
