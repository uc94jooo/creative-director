# Unlimited Youth (Nike · 2016)

## Insight
The third dimension of the Unlimited platform: youth not as a chronological fact but as a quality of engagement — the athlete who refuses to stop regardless of age. "Unlimited" transcends age when it is a mindset rather than a physical condition.

## Mechanic
A film in the Unlimited series focusing on older athletes who continue to compete and train — inverting the cultural assumption that sport belongs to the young. The story of the veteran athlete who refuses the limits of age is a counterpoint to the youth-focused "Unlimited Future."

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16): the emotional power of an older athlete defying age requires a patient buildup; the payoff is earned. Truth telling (P13): the athletes shown are real; their achievements are documented. The platform's flexibility (future/you/youth as three angles on the same idea) demonstrates how a strong platform generates distinct executions without losing coherence.

## Steal
A platform idea strong enough to address youth, maturity, and age simultaneously is a platform worth investing in. Build the platform first, then let different executions find different audience segments without contradiction.
