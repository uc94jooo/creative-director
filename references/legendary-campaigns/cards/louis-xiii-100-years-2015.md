# 100 Years (Louis XIII · 2015)

## Insight
Louis XIII cognac takes 100 years to make. Any brand claim about patience or craft is undermined by conventional advertising's immediacy. The only honest way to communicate a century-long product is to create communication that cannot be consumed for a century.

## Mechanic
John Malkovich starred in a film about 2115 that was locked in a fireproof safe and will only be screened on November 18, 2115. Only 1,000 tickets were issued — to people who will almost certainly be dead when the film premieres. The trailer was released publicly.

## Why it worked
The mechanic IS the message. A brand that will not reveal its film for 100 years demonstrates, rather than claims, that it thinks in century-long terms. The inaccessibility creates desire. The absurdist elegance generates global press coverage for a product that almost no one can afford.

## Steal
Find the one structural commitment that cannot be faked. Not a claim — a lock, a contract, a physical restriction. The constraint becomes the communication.
