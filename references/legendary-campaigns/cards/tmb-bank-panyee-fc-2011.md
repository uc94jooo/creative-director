# Panyee FC (TMB Bank · 2011)

## Insight
Believing in your own potential — regardless of your starting conditions — is not natural or easy. It is a choice made despite evidence. A bank that finances small businesses understands this better than anyone.

## Mechanic
A documentary-style film telling the true story of a football team from Koh Panyee — a floating village in Thailand with no land — who built a floating football pitch from waste wood and competed in the regional championships. The film ends with a call to action about TMB Bank supporting people who dare.

## Why it worked
The story was real, which meant it required no exaggeration. The brief — a bank brief, the most generic brief in advertising — was answered with something genuinely moving because the story did the work. The brand's connection (we believe in people who try against impossible odds) was earned by the specificity of the case.

## Steal
Documentary truth in advertising is more persuasive than any fictional narrative because it cannot be dismissed as brand fantasy. Find the case study that proves your brand's point so specifically and so completely that no script could improve on it.
