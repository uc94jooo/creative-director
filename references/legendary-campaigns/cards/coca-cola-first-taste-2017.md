# First Taste (Coca-Cola · 2017)

## Insight
The first taste of Coca-Cola is unforgettable — but it is also unrepeatable. Every subsequent taste measures itself against that first experience. The emotional power of the brand lives in the memory of the first encounter.

## Mechanic
A film exploring the emotional experience of encountering Coca-Cola for the first time — told across different people in different situations, with the shared recognition of that specific sensory memory as the connecting thread.

## Why it worked
P16 (long-form drama) building emotional territory around the product's most powerful brand truth: the sensory memory of first experience. P09 (user as co-author) through the shared recognition mechanic — audiences recall their own first taste and project it onto the film. The campaign activates personal memory without needing to manufacture a new one.

## Steal
Every product has a "first encounter" that its most loyal users remember. Making that memory the content of a campaign activates a personal archive that no other brand can access. You are not creating an emotion; you are unlocking one that already exists.
