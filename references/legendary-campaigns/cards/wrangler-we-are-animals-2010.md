# We Are Animals (Wrangler · 2010)

## Insight
Denim is the fabric of physical life — outdoor work, rough terrain, bodies in motion. Fashion advertising sanitises this into stylised editorial. Wrangler's truth is rawer: the body doing real things in demanding conditions is the brand's natural territory.

## Mechanic
Print and OOH campaign featuring raw photography of human bodies in extreme physical contact with nature — in mud, water, wind, dust — with the line "We Are Animals." No studio, no styling, no perfection. The photography quality elevated the rawness to art.

## Why it worked
The contrast between fashion advertising's usual pristine ideal and Wrangler's deliberately raw, physical imagery was itself the positioning. "We Are Animals" as a tagline reclaimed the animality that fashion had polished away. Cannes Gold suggests the craft matched the ambition.

## Steal
When a brand's authentic territory is rawness and the category norm is aspiration, commit entirely to the raw. Half-raw (some grit, some shine) reads as confused. The photography must be as committed as the claim.
