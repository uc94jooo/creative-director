# The Gene Project (Marmite · 2018)

## Insight
Marmite's "love it or hate it" positioning is so strongly held that consumers believe their preference is innate — perhaps even genetic.

## Mechanic
Marmite partnered with a genetics company to create a real DNA test that could indicate predisposition to loving or hating Marmite. Consumers submitted saliva samples; results were delivered with personalised Marmite-branded reports. PR expanded the debate about taste genetics.

## Why it worked
Taking the brand's oldest claim ("love it or hate it") and making it literally, scientifically testable was absurdist at the level of an idea but entirely real as an execution. The product turned a slogan into a biological fact-finding exercise, generating massive earned media.

## Steal
When your brand has a claim that audiences accept as a joke, make it literally, verifiably true. The joke becomes proof, and proof generates media at a fraction of paid advertising cost.
