# Home (Nike · 2022)

## Insight
America's Black athletes are celebrated as representatives of a country that frequently excludes them from the full experience of citizenship. The tension between representing a nation and living in it is the insight no sports brand addresses directly.

## Mechanic
A film exploring what "home" means to Black American athletes — moving between sports achievement, community, family, and the ambivalence of national identity. P13 (truth-telling) without resolution: the film holds the tension rather than offering a patriotic answer.

## Why it worked
Nike has built enough credibility through Dream Crazy and Dream Crazier to address this tension without it reading as exploitation. The film earns its emotional weight by refusing to resolve the contradiction — home is complicated, and the campaign says so. P16 (long-form drama) gives space for that complexity without reducing it to a slogan.

## Steal
When a tension has no resolution, don't resolve it. Holding the contradiction honestly is more emotionally resonant — and more credible — than offering a false reconciliation. The brand that says "it's complicated" earns more trust than the one that says "and yet we believe in America."
