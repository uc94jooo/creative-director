# Sway (Apple · 2016)

## Insight

Dance is the physical expression of music — when sound is perfect, the body responds without thinking. Apple's audio products enable the purest connection between music and movement.

## Mechanic

Film showcasing extraordinary dance performance set to music through Apple devices — high-craft choreography and cinematography where the movement itself is the product demonstration of audio quality.

## Why it worked

Craft as message: the dancer's precision and expressiveness transferred as implied product quality without any spoken claim. Apple's extended film series used craft excellence as the argument.

## Steal

When you cannot directly demonstrate a sensory product benefit, find the human behavior that emerges from it at its most extreme and beautiful. Film that behavior.
