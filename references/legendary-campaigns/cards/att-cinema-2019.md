# Cinema (AT&T · 2019)

## Insight
Connection — AT&T's product category — is most meaningful in the moments where two people are brought together in ways that would otherwise be impossible. Cinema, as a shared collective experience, is one of the few places where strangers still sit together in the dark and feel the same thing at the same time.

## Mechanic
A long-form film using cinema as a metaphor for human connection — the shared emotional experience of a film in a theatre as a demonstration of what genuine connection feels like. High production, cinematic quality that was designed to be shown in the cinema context it depicted.

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16) allowed AT&T to move away from telecom category communication (speed, coverage, price) and occupy a cultural space around connection as an emotional rather than a technical concept. The format matched the subject — a cinema ad about cinema.

## Steal
When a brand's core concept (connection, communication, movement) can be demonstrated through a cultural form rather than a product demonstration, use the cultural form. The medium proves the message without saying it.
