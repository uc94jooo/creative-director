# The Everyday Tactician (Xbox · 2024)

## Insight
Football Manager players spend thousands of hours making tactical decisions that are identical in structure to what real football club analysts do — but the game world and the professional world are completely separate. The gap between the simulation and the reality is both the audience's fantasy and Xbox's creative brief.

## Mechanic
Xbox and Football Manager partnered with Bromley FC to offer an actual in-game Football Manager player a genuine data analyst role at the real club — assessing real candidates on the strength of their in-game decisions. One person crossed from the simulation to the reality. The competition was played out as a campaign, with updates and content throughout.

## Why it worked
P09 (user as co-author): the audience's existing gameplay became the job application. P18 (tech as canvas): Football Manager's simulation technology is the platform that makes the real-world role possible. The campaign dissolved the boundary between game and reality — not as a gimmick, but as a genuine career opportunity. Contagious featured it as campaign of the week for demonstrating that gaming audiences can be reached through meaningful acts, not just advertising.

## Steal
When your product is a simulation of a real-world activity, find the moment where the simulation and the reality can genuinely intersect. The proof that your simulation is credible is that the real world will accept its output.
