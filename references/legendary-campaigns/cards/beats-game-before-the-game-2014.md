# The Game Before the Game (Beats by Dre · 2014)

## Insight
Before a World Cup match, footballers put on headphones — an intimate, private ritual that cameras never follow. The product is already there; the brand just needed to show what it carries.

## Mechanic
A five-minute film for the 2014 World Cup featuring Neymar, Suárez, and other stars in the hours before kick-off: phone calls from mothers, rituals, pressure, silence. Headphones on throughout. No explicit product mention.

## Why it worked
Beats inserted itself into an authentic pre-existing ritual without explaining the product. Association with the most emotionally charged sporting moment of the year required only showing what already happens — no fabrication.

## Steal
If your product is already part of an existing ritual, film the ritual, not the product. The brand earns the association without claiming it.
