# Happy Birthday From Earth (Husqvarna · 2023)

## Insight
Garden tools are bought for the garden, but the garden's relationship to the planet is rarely made explicit. Earth Day is the annual moment when that connection surfaces — but most brands use it for corporate messaging, not for inviting real participation.

## Mechanic
An integrated campaign combining participatory elements (P09) with a seasonal ritual structure (P10) — building a collective "birthday gift" to Earth using the audience's own outdoor spaces and Husqvarna's products as the medium. User-generated green action created the campaign content.

## Why it worked
Husqvarna's core product (outdoor power tools for gardens and lawns) connects directly to the planet's green space. P09 and P10 together create a repeatable annual ritual: people are invited to participate with what they already own, and the campaign grows with each year's edition.

## Steal
When your product literally touches the natural world, use that physical connection as proof of environmental commitment — and turn it into an annual participatory act rather than a one-off statement.
