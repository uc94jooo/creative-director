# Shade Running (Nike · 2012)

## Insight
Running in extreme heat is a challenge most runners face but no sports brand addresses directly. The adaptation strategies runners develop — hunting shade, running at dawn — are evidence of genuine athletic commitment overlooked by aspirational advertising.

## Mechanic
A film celebrating the specific, unglamorous tactics of runners adapting to heat — running through shade patches, choosing routes by cover, starting before sunrise. The ordinary difficulty of real runners is treated as athletic achievement.

## Why it worked
By focusing on a specific, real running challenge rather than an aspirational race or record, the film earns trust with actual runners while communicating Nike's understanding of their lived experience. Specificity is empathy.

## Steal
Find the most specific, unglamorous version of your audience's engagement with the product. Celebrate that, not the aspirational version. The specific truth is always more credible than the aspirational lie.
