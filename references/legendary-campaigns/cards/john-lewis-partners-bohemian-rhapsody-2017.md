# Bohemian Rhapsody (John Lewis & Partners · 2017)

## Insight
Christmas is the annual occasion when families who have become strangers to each other's daily lives are briefly reunited — and must navigate the awkward and tender recovery of closeness.

## Mechanic
A film depicting various family moments of connection — a Maison de Mogwai cover of Bohemian Rhapsody as the soundtrack — anchored in the theme of unlikely togetherness. Partnership with Moz the Monster character running parallel to the main campaign.

## Why it worked
The combination of an unexpected soundtrack choice with emotionally authentic family vignettes maintained tonal distinctiveness within the established John Lewis register. The monster character added a separate engagement layer for children.

## Steal
Multi-audience campaigns work when the adult and child layers are genuine rather than bolted together. Give children their own story; give adults theirs; let the brand be the connective tissue.
