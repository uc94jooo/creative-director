# Life's Adventure (Leroy Merlin · 2018)

## Insight
Home improvement isn't about the tools or materials — it's about what people build their lives into. The store is never the point; the life being constructed is.

## Mechanic
A long-form film following characters across significant life moments — the home as the stage for human drama. The home improvement retailer is present only through the backdrop of spaces being transformed and lives being lived in them.

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16) allows a DIY retailer to escape functional product territory entirely and occupy emotional territory — belonging, aspiration, growth — that makes the brand feel like a life partner rather than a hardware store. The product is invisible; the promise is everything.

## Steal
Retail brands can exit product communication entirely through long-form storytelling that positions them as enablers of life chapters rather than sellers of goods. The store becomes a supporting character, never the protagonist.
