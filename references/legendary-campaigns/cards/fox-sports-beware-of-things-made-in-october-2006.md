# Beware of Things Made in October (Fox Sports · 2006)

## Insight
October is baseball playoffs month — the moment when America is distracted by the sport. The campaign capitalized on this cultural truth: October is when things get decided, when drama peaks, when attention is captured.

## Mechanic
A campaign using absurdist humor (P11) and cultural hijack (P05) around October as a month of significance, tying the Fox Sports baseball broadcast to a broader cultural moment of "October intensity."

## Why it worked
Sports broadcasting is at its strongest when it claims ownership of a cultural moment rather than just broadcasting games. Tying the brand to October's cultural intensity (elections, harvest, baseball) gave Fox Sports a broader claim than just live sports coverage.

## Steal
A sports broadcaster's most powerful positioning is not "watch our games" but "this is the cultural moment." Own the moment, not just the content.
