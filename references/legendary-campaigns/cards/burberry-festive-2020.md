# Festive (Burberry · 2020)

## Insight
The Christmas festive film is a ritual in British fashion and retail — the audience expects it annually. Burberry used this serialised expectation (P10) to anchor its brand in a seasonal cultural moment.

## Mechanic
A cinematic Christmas film, likely featuring ensemble cast in classic British winter settings, with Burberry outerwear as the visual anchor. Extended format typical of luxury brand festive content.

## Why it worked
The long-form drama format (P16) allows emotional accumulation across a narrative, while the seasonal ritual mechanic (P10) means the audience returns expecting an emotional payoff. Luxury brands that commit to annual festive storytelling build earned anticipation — each year's film arrives with pre-existing interest.

## Steal
Commit to a seasonal creative ritual before the first execution lands. The invariant element (annual release window, brand tone, format length) is as important as the story. Audiences need to know what to expect in order to anticipate it.
