# Great Shows Stay With You (Amazon Prime · 2022)

## Insight
In a world of content abundance, the problem is not access — it is emotional connection. The shows that matter are the ones that leave a trace: a scene you replay, a character you grieve, a line you quote years later. That trace is the product.

## Mechanic
Campaign films depicting how great television moments physically stay with viewers — characters from beloved shows appearing in their everyday lives, haunting them in the best possible way. Long-form drama (P16) executes the emotional logic of the insight.

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16): the extended runtime allows genuine emotional investment rather than a snapshot. The insight is true — great shows do stay with you — and the mechanic literalises that truth visually. User as co-author (P09): viewers recognize their own relationship with beloved shows in the portrayal.

## Steal
When selling a content platform, sell the emotional residue of the content, not the content itself. The memory is more valuable to the audience than the catalogue. Make them remember something they felt rather than describe something they could watch.
