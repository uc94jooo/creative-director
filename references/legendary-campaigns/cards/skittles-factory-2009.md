# Factory (Skittles · 2009)

## Insight
The origin of Skittles — where do they come from? The "Factory" spot presumably extends Skittles' absurdist logic to answer that question in characteristically impossible terms.

## Mechanic
A short absurdist film set in or involving a factory in Skittles' surrealist creative framework. Exact execution details require source verification.

## Why it worked
Factory as concept provides Skittles' absurdism with a new arena: origin mythology. The brand's internal logic can be applied to answer "where do they come from?" in a way that extends rather than explains.

## Steal
Brand origin stories don't need to be factual to be effective. An absurdist origin that follows brand logic can be more memorable and more brand-building than a true story told plainly.
