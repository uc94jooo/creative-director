# Legendary Posters (Heineken · 2014)

## Insight
Great moments in sport and culture deserve better than forgetting. Heineken, associated with premium occasions and legendary experiences, has standing to claim ownership of "legendary" — but only if it can demonstrate that claim through something beyond a tagline.

## Mechanic
A series of OOH posters designed to look like legendary moments from sporting or cultural history, rendered in a distinctive graphic style that positions Heineken as the appropriate accompaniment to legendary occasions. The design language is the argument.

## Why it worked
The poster format (P17: Design as Idea) does the work that a film would explain: the visual composition directly associates Heineken with cultural legend without requiring text to make the claim. The invisible brand (P07) technique — minimal product presence, maximum context — signals brand confidence.

## Steal
When you want to own an abstract quality (legend, prestige, greatness), don't claim it in copy. Design a visual system that demonstrates it. Let the aesthetic do the argument that the words would diminish.
