# Confusing Times (Burger King · 2020)

## Insight
2020 was universally described as unprecedented, confusing, and chaotic. Rather than pivot to seriousness, Burger King chose to acknowledge the chaos with characteristic absurdism — the brand's cultural position is irreverence, not earnestness.

## Mechanic
A series of Burger King communications during 2020 used the language of "confusing times" to frame their promotions, menu items, and brand communications through the lens of collective cultural bewilderment. The campaign acknowledged the moment without exploiting it.

## Why it worked
Cultural hijack (P05) that doesn't overreach: Burger King claimed the cultural moment of shared confusion without attaching it to tragedy. The absurdist brand voice (P11) makes acknowledgment of chaos feel like the brand's natural register rather than crisis communication.

## Steal
In cultural moments where every brand is pivoting to seriousness, consider whether your brand's authentic voice is earnest or ironic. If it's ironic, staying ironic during a serious moment is more honest than performing seriousness.
