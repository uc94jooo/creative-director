# Keep the World Flawed (Diesel · 2018)

## Insight
Second year of Diesel's flaw platform. The evolution: from "embrace your flaw" (personal) to "keep the world flawed" (collective, political). A world of perfected surfaces, algorithmic smoothing, and filter culture needs an argument for preserving imperfection at civilisational scale.

## Mechanic
A campaign extending the "Go With the Flaw" platform from individual identity to a collective stance: imperfection is not just a personal liberation but a cultural necessity. The flaw-as-resistance applied to the world, not just the self.

## Why it worked
P03 (behavior inversion): where fashion increasingly sells digitally-perfected aspiration, Diesel escalates its counter-position. P16 gives the expansion room. The platform growth from personal to political is correct — "Go With the Flaw" established the personal case; "Keep the World Flawed" makes the stakes larger, which is the right direction for a fashion brand trying to sustain cultural relevance across multiple seasons.

## Steal
When a single-season campaign works, the extension should escalate the stakes, not just repeat the insight. Move the argument from personal to cultural, from individual to collective — that's the difference between a campaign and a platform.
