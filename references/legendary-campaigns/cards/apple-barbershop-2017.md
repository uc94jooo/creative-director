# Barbershop (Apple · 2017)

## Insight

Music is the social glue of community spaces — a barbershop's rhythm is set as much by what's playing as who's talking. The right song turns any space into a shared experience.

## Mechanic

Short film set in a barbershop where AirPods and Apple Music transform the daily rhythm of the space — absurdist musical sequences erupt from ordinary haircut moments, linking personal listening to communal energy.

## Why it worked

Long-form drama meets absurdism: the barbershop setting gave the musical absurdity communal warmth rather than alienating weirdness. Apple's approach in this era used cultural community contexts (barbershops, churches) to embed technology in Black cultural spaces authentically.

## Steal

Ground technological product benefits in a specific community space where those benefits are already practiced informally. The specificity earns cultural credibility that generic lifestyle advertising cannot.
