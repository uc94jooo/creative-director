# Spreadbeats (Spotify · 2025)

## Insight

Spotify's advertising pitch deck is a boring spreadsheet — the most uncreative format possible for the most creative medium. What if the pitch deck itself became the product demo?

## Mechanic

Spotify presented its advertising capabilities to media buyers as a fully interactive spreadsheet (Google Sheets) where every cell was a clickable music experience — the spreadsheet became a musical instrument demonstrating Spotify's audio targeting, playlist context, and emotional range.

## Why it worked

Tech as canvas meets design as idea: the format inversion (spreadsheet as music player) was the demo. B2B advertising typically ignores the format of its communication. By making the format the message, Spotify turned a sales document into a talked-about creative piece.

## Steal

In B2B contexts, the format of your pitch is almost always ignored. Find a format that is the opposite of what's expected, then make that format demonstrate the product. The surprise is disproportionate because expectations are so low.
