# Keep the World Flawed (Levi's · 2017)

## Insight

People who wear Levi's resist the pressure to conform to perfection; authenticity means embracing imperfection as a badge of character rather than something to fix.

## Mechanic

Long-form film celebrating imperfect people, imperfect moments, and the rough edges that define real lives — positioning Levi's denim as the fabric of genuine, unpolished humanity.

## Why it worked

Behavior inversion in a category obsessed with aspiration and polish: Levi's took the opposite stance, making "flawed" a desired state rather than something to overcome. The emotional territory of defiant authenticity is ownable for a heritage denim brand.

## Steal

Name a flaw your audience secretly wears as a badge of pride. Make the brand the one that celebrates it without irony. Works best for heritage brands with authenticity credentials.
