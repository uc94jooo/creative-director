# Pub Museums (Heineken · 2022)

## Insight

Historic Irish pubs are disappearing — they carry irreplaceable community memory, but no institution protects them. Heineken, as a pub brand, has authentic standing to act.

## Mechanic

Heineken turned endangered historic Irish pubs into official "museums," registering them as cultural heritage sites with artifacts, plaques, and preservation documentation — making them literally worth protecting.

## Why it worked

Serialization and ritual meets user participation: the campaign created an ongoing ritual of discovery and nomination, giving drinkers agency in preserving something they already loved. The action (preservation) was more valuable than any ad.

## Steal

If your brand is linked to a physical space that is disappearing, make preservation the campaign. The brand becomes the protector, not the promoter.
