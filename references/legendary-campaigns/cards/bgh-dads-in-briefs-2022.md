# Dads in Briefs (BGH · 2022)

## Insight

Air conditioners are a rational purchase — nobody finds the category exciting. But sweaty summer heat is something everyone dreads, and the most relatable victims of heat are dads who suffer without air conditioning.

## Mechanic

OOH print campaign featuring sweaty middle-aged dads in briefs — the most unappealing, authentic image of what life without air conditioning looks like — as the hero image. No aspirational lifestyle. Just the honest, uncomfortable consequence of not buying the product.

## Why it worked

Absurdism meets design as idea: the anti-aspirational visual was the idea. In a category full of cool blue imagery and family comfort scenes, a sweating dad in underwear was impossible to ignore. The ugliness was the argument.

## Steal

In rational categories with interchangeable products, find the most honest negative consequence of NOT having your product. Make that consequence the hero image. Anti-aspiration is more memorable than aspiration.
