# Protection (Volkswagen · 2012)

## Insight
VW safety features (airbags, crumple zones, collision sensors) are technical advantages that drivers can't feel or see until they need them. Communicating safety through statistics is forgettable; demonstrating safety through impact is visceral but ethically constrained.

## Mechanic
An absurdist demonstration of VW's safety/protection features — likely an extreme scenario where the protection technology performs beyond reasonable expectation, delivered with comedic exaggeration rather than crash-test gravity.

## Why it worked
Safety advertising typically relies on fear (show a crash) or statistics (show a test result). P11 + P14 suggests VW found a way to demonstrate the protection attribute through absurd extremity — making the safety feature memorable through comedy rather than fear.

## Steal
Safety features are unappreciated until needed. To make them desirable rather than merely reassuring, demonstrate them in scenarios extreme enough to be absurd but logical enough to prove the claim.
