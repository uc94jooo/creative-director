# This is SportsCenter — Best-of Compilation (ESPN · 1995–ongoing)

## Insight
The "This is SportsCenter" campaign's longevity means it functions as a cultural archive of sports history. The best-of list is itself a media artifact — evidence that a campaign became a canon.

## Mechanic
A curated compilation of the campaign's most memorable spots, surfaced through editorial lists and fan recommendations. Individual spots within the series range from legendary (LeBron, Manning) to obscure, but the aggregate forms a portrait of American sports culture across three decades.

## Why it worked
Compilations extend campaign life through nostalgia and discovery. Each list surfaces the campaign to non-traditional audiences and gives current fans access to spots that predate their fandom. The format itself becomes another distribution mechanism.

## Steal
Build campaigns designed to be anthologized. Each execution should be independently shareable and strong enough to stand without the series. The series then aggregates into a cultural record.
