# After Hours Athlete (Jameson · 2014)

## Insight
The real sporting arena for many young adults is the bar — the endurance, the strategy, the camaraderie. Celebrating "after hours" performance with the same reverence as athletic achievement reframes drinking as its own kind of sport.

## Mechanic
Long-form film series treating late-night socialising as athletic performance — complete with sports documentary aesthetics, slow-motion, and commentary. Jameson whiskey as the fuel for this alternative athletic universe.

## Why it worked
Sports documentary tropes are instantly legible and emotionally loaded. Applying them to the opposite context (bar nights, not stadiums) creates gentle absurdist comedy while genuinely flattering the target audience's identity.

## Steal
Borrow the visual grammar of a prestigious, emotionally loaded genre and apply it to an everyday, low-stakes context your audience inhabits. The contrast creates warmth without condescension.
