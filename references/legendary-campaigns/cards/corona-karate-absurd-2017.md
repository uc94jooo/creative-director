# Karate (Corona · 2017)

## Insight
Part of the same Corona absurdist series — the brand's relaxation mythology colliding with unexpected physical disciplines. Karate's formality and aggression are the perfect opposite of Corona's ease and passivity.

## Mechanic
Absurdist film placing Corona's sensory language alongside karate — the incongruity of beach relaxation and martial discipline played for comic effect within the brand's visual world.

## Why it worked
Execution within a series built on a single absurdist engine: Corona's established mythology + incongruous context = comedy. The further the displacement from beach relaxation, the greater the contrast and the sharper the brand reinforcement.

## Steal
Series comedy benefits from escalating incongruity — each entry should push the contrast further than the last. Map your brand's core sensory territory, then find the most opposite context and put them in the same frame.
