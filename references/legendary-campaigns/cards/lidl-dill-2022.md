# DILL (LIDL · 2022)

## Insight
Dill is Sweden's most culturally loaded herb — beloved, omnipresent, slightly absurd in its ubiquity. LIDL, as a discount retailer trying to earn Swedish cultural legitimacy, could stake a claim to this humble, ridiculous piece of Swedish identity.

## Mechanic
A film treating LIDL's dill — packaged, sold, Swedish — with the gravity and production quality of a luxury brand launch. Documentary-style reverence applied to a 15-kronor bunch of herbs. Design-driven: the typography and visual identity of the film were themselves the joke.

## Why it worked
The contrast between prestige execution (luxury brand launch aesthetics) and humble subject (discount store dill) was the entire concept. By treating Sweden's cheapest herb as Sweden's most important cultural export, LIDL positioned itself as a brand with genuine Swedish cultural fluency rather than just competitive prices.

## Steal
When a budget brand needs cultural credibility, find the unpretentious, humble, locally specific thing the category ignores and elevate it with maximum craft. The gap between production quality and subject matter is the joke, the insight, and the brand positioning simultaneously.
