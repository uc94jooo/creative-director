# Hook (Volvo Trucks · 2014)

## Insight
Truck load-securing technology can be demonstrated through the most extreme cargo possible — a human body.

## Mechanic
Rock legend, stuntman, or extreme subject is secured only by Volvo's hook and fastening system while the truck performs maneuvers — demonstrating load-securing precision through implicit human risk. Part of the Volvo Trucks "Live Test" film series.

## Why it worked
Each film in the series raises the stakes of the demonstration. Hook uses implied personal danger to make the product claim visceral. The format consistency (unexpected demonstrator + extreme condition + real truck) maintains brand coherence across the franchise.

## Steal
In a demonstration franchise, vary the demonstrator and the condition while holding the format constant. The invariant structure makes each execution feel familiar; the variable makes each one shareable independently.
