# Bounce (Apple · 2019)

## Insight

The right music makes even ordinary movements feel like performance — joy is physically expressed through rhythm and motion when you're fully connected to sound.

## Mechanic

Film featuring a young woman bouncing through Hong Kong streets, with every bounce synced to the music playing through AirPods — the physicality of joy made literal and contagious.

## Why it worked

Apple's craft-as-message series continued with choreographic precision: the Hong Kong setting provided visual density and energy, the physics of bouncing made musical rhythm tangible. Energy transfer from sound to body to viewer.

## Steal

Music product films that literalize the physical effect of sound on the body are more memorable than those that demonstrate features. Find the most joyful physical behavior your product enables. Film it without explanation.
