# Masterpiece (Coca-Cola · 2023)

## Insight
Great art and a cold Coca-Cola share the same quality — the power to transport you from the mundane present into a moment of pure feeling.

## Mechanic
A film where a student in an art gallery sees artworks come to life and a Coca-Cola bottle pass between famous paintings — Van Gogh, Warhol, Modigliani, Hokusai — each rendered in the artist's authentic style through a combination of live action and animation.

## Why it worked
The craft itself made the argument: a brand that can commission this quality of animation and art direction earns its association with masterpiece-level experience. The product barely appears; the feeling does all the work.

## Steal
Position your brand alongside the highest-status cultural references without claiming to be equivalent. The juxtaposition transfers prestige; the craft proves the brand belongs in the conversation.
