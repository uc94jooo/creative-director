# Man Boobs (MACMA · 2015)

## Insight
Facebook's content policy prohibits showing female nipples but permits male nipples. A breast self-examination tutorial was removed from the platform because it showed breasts. Replacing female breasts with male breasts would make the content platform-compliant while exposing the policy's absurdity.

## Mechanic
MACMA filmed a breast self-examination tutorial using a man's body — specifically a man's chest — to demonstrate the technique. Because the nipples were male, Facebook could not remove the video. The tutorial was medically accurate and fully accessible. It spread globally.

## Why it worked
The absurdist loophole (P11) is the only logical response to an absurd platform policy. The truth-telling (P13) is doubly layered: the medical truth (how to self-examine) and the social truth (content policies have gender-based double standards). The mechanic required no budget and generated global press coverage.

## Steal
When a platform's rules work against your public health or social mission, find the literal loophole. Exploit it completely and publicly. The exploitation IS the argument.
