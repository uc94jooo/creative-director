# World's Biggest Asshole (Donate Life America · 2016)

## Insight
Organ donor registration feels morally heavy and bureaucratically dull — the message attracts only the already-converted. But even the worst person can save four lives.

## Mechanic
A film tracking the most objectionable man imaginable — he lies, steals, insults everyone — who dies in a brawl. Final card: "Coleman donated his organs and saved 4 lives. He may have been the world's biggest asshole — and the world's biggest hero."

## Why it worked
The moral inversion completely disarms the "I'm not good enough to be a donor" barrier. Registration is democratized: even a terrible person qualifies. Absurdism lowers defenses so the message lands without preachiness.

## Steal
Remove the moral bar from a behaviour-change ask. Show that even the least qualified person can do it — this inverts the usual "good people do X" frame and makes the action feel accessible to everyone.
