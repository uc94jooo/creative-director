# Appreciation (HBO Go · 2014)

## Insight
Sharing a genuinely appreciated HBO show with someone who doesn't connect with it in the same way creates a specific loneliness — you wanted to share something meaningful and it failed.

## Mechanic
A short film in the "Awkward Family Viewing" series depicting the discomfort of watching something you love with someone who doesn't appreciate it — the reverse of standard awkward viewing, where the content itself is uncomfortable. HBO Go becomes the solution: watch alone and keep the experience intact.

## Why it worked
The series closure adds a final dimension: it's not just the content that's awkward — the mismatch between viewer and co-viewer about what's valuable is itself uncomfortable. The campaign covers both directions of the problem.

## Steal
In a campaign series, include the inverse of the core scenario. The same product can solve the same problem from opposite directions — both "I don't want to watch this with them" and "they don't want to watch this with me."
