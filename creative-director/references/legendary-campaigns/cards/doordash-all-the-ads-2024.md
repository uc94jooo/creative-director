# DoorDash-All-the-Ads (DoorDash · 2024)

## Insight
Super Bowl ads spend millions to offer viewers fantasy. DoorDash offered to actually deliver the products from every advertiser's spot — turning the cultural ritual of watching Super Bowl ads into a shopping event with a single promo code.

## Mechanic
DoorDash ran one Super Bowl spot that offered a promo code containing every product advertised during the game — from M&Ms to Budweiser to Nerds — in one giant delivery order. The promo code itself ("DOOORDASH" with extra Os, hidden in a rapid-fire visual sequence) was a puzzle audiences had to solve in real time. The campaign ran as an integrated digital + broadcast event.

## Why it worked
P05 (cultural hijack) of the Super Bowl advertising ecosystem — DoorDash turned every other brand's expensive media buy into free creative material for its own campaign. P11 (absurd as carrier) via the impossibly large delivery order as comedy. P02 (enemy or conflict) redefined competitors as complements — the more valuable the other brands' ads, the more valuable the DoorDash offer. The promo code mechanic created active participation during a passive viewing event.

## Steal
When your category is full of competitors, make their marketing budgets work for you. If your delivery or distribution covers what others advertise, you can turn their spend into your proof of relevance.
