# Unlock (Apple · 2018)

## Insight

Technology should remove friction from moments that matter — not add it. The iPhone is a key that opens possibility rather than a device that complicates access.

## Mechanic

Short film showing how Face ID and iPhone features unlock real-life moments — presented as a series of human scenarios where the phone removes barriers and opens doors, literal and metaphorical.

## Why it worked

Long-form drama applied to product: Apple's extended film format let the metaphor of "unlocking" breathe across multiple story beats, making a functional feature feel emotionally meaningful.

## Steal

Take a product's most utilitarian function. Find the human metaphor that underlies it. Build a film around the metaphor rather than the feature.
