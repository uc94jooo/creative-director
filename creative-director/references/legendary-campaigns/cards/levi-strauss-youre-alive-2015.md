# You're Alive (Levi's · 2015)

## Insight

TODO: Year inferred; verify against campaign records.

Youth culture is about being fully present — taking risks, feeling everything, wearing your life on your body. Denim is the armor and the record of that aliveness.

## Mechanic

Long-form film celebrating youth, risk-taking, and authentic experience — the energy of being alive expressed through the physical reality of living in Levi's.

## Why it worked

Long-form drama: Levi's youth positioning requires credibility beyond product claims. Films that celebrate the aliveness of the audience rather than the quality of the jeans sidestep rational comparison and occupy emotional territory.

## Steal

In fashion, the audience's aspirations are always more interesting than the product's features. Make the audience the hero. The product is just what they wear while they're being heroic.
