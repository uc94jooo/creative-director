# Stop Working Out in the Past (Tonal · 2025)

## Insight

Traditional gym equipment — dumbbells, barbells, weight machines — is literally designed in the past and hasn't fundamentally evolved in decades. Using old technology is working out in the past.

## Mechanic

Film using bold design and absurdist visual language to present traditional gym equipment as comically outdated — weight plates and dumbbells styled as primitive artifacts, contrasted with Tonal's modern smart-weight system as the obvious future.

## Why it worked

Design as idea meets absurdism: the concept was self-contained and visually executable without explanation. The phrase "Stop Working Out in the Past" reframed the competitive landscape — every competitor's product became evidence of the claim.

## Steal

Reframe your entire competitive set as being from the past. If your product genuinely represents a category step-change, make the obsolescence of everything else the campaign rather than listing your own features.
