# Adoptable (Pedigree · 2024)

## Insight
Shelter dogs are photographed with basic smartphones in poor lighting and returned to shelters at a rate of one in five adoptions — partly because the presentation fails to show who the dog actually is. Pedigree already runs digital advertising. Every dollar spent on a Pedigree ad could simultaneously feature a real adoptable dog from a nearby shelter. The only barrier is the gap in visual quality between a shelter phone photo and a studio production shot.

## Mechanic
Pedigree and Nexus Studios built an AI model that transforms basic shelter dog photographs into studio-quality photography, replicating the exact visual composition and nuance of each individual dog — fur length, body shape, color identity, and unique markings — at production standard. The generated image can be inserted into any live Pedigree digital ad in any format. Each ad links to a personalized landing page for that specific dog. When a dog is adopted, it is automatically removed from media rotation. Geographic data and household profile matching connects dogs to the locations most likely to suit them — proximity to parks, household size — improving match quality and reducing returns. Every Pedigree media dollar now doubles as adoption advertising.

## Why it worked
P07 (Utility as Brand): the advertising system itself becomes an adoption platform — the brand's purpose isn't communicated by the ad, it's executed by it. P12 (Data Made Human): geographic matching and AI photography translate abstract data into individual dogs with faces, stories, and addresses. P17 (Cause as Core): Pedigree's activist brand purpose — fighting for every dog — is operationalized at the infrastructure level rather than expressed at the campaign level. The idea scales with the media spend: every additional dollar creates another adoption opportunity.

## Steal
When your advertising has a canvas (digital out-of-home, display, social), ask what social purpose could be embedded directly into that canvas rather than alongside it. The strongest purpose execution makes the advertising itself do the work — not a separate campaign about the work.
