# Pinterest Yard Sale (Krylon · 2013)

## Insight
People pin DIY inspiration on Pinterest constantly but rarely act on it. Krylon spray paint is the tool that closes the gap between pinned aspiration and real transformation.

## Mechanic
Krylon created the "first ever Pinterest yard sale" — buying cheap yard sale items, spray painting them in Krylon colours, pinning the before/after transformations, and selling the upgraded items. Each pin linked directly to the product used.

## Why it worked
The mechanic was native to Pinterest's format and psychology. It demonstrated product efficacy through real transformation, not claims. The social loop (pin → buy → transform → re-pin) was self-sustaining and generated genuine product proof.

## Steal
Build your campaign inside a platform's native behaviour loop rather than advertising on top of it. When the campaign format is indistinguishable from organic platform use, it earns rather than interrupts attention.
