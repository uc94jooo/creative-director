# Lost Barrel (Johnnie Walker · 2013)

## Insight
Whisky is a product of time — barrels lost or forgotten for decades emerge as treasures. The "lost barrel" narrative embodies the brand's "Keep Walking" philosophy: patience, resilience, and the belief that time transforms.

## Mechanic
A long-form cinematic film (P16) narrating the discovery or journey of a lost whisky barrel, using the craft of storytelling and cinematography (P08) to communicate Johnnie Walker's heritage and values. Scotland and time are the visual territory.

## Why it worked
The whisky category's strongest emotional territory is heritage, craft, and the passage of time. P16 long-form film and P08 (craft as message) are the natural format for that story — a short ad cannot convey decades of patience. The "lost barrel" narrative is a specific, concrete embodiment of abstract values.

## Steal
When your brand's promise is inherently long-duration (patience, heritage, craft), find a story that makes that duration physical and specific — a barrel, a building, a person — rather than abstract.
