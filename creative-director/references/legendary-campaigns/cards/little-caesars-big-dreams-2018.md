# Big Dreams (Little Caesars · 2018)

## Insight
Little Caesars' Hot-N-Ready value proposition is unambiguous: cheap, fast, ready. Rather than apologize for the positioning, lean into absurdist humor that celebrates the simple joy of accessible pizza.

## Mechanic
A comedic film in which characters articulate hilariously mundane or absurdly deflated "big dreams" — the joke being the gap between the grandiosity implied by the phrase and the small, affordable satisfaction the product delivers.

## Why it worked
Absurd as carrier (P11): the joke lives in the gap between inflated expectation (big dreams) and humble, satisfying reality (Hot-N-Ready pizza). The brand is honest about what it is and makes that honesty funny rather than defensive.

## Steal
Own your price-value positioning with humor rather than defensiveness. The absurdist framing makes affordability charming rather than cheap. Honest positioning + comedy = brand confidence.
