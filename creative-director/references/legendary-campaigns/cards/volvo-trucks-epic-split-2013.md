# The Epic Split (Volvo Trucks · 2013)

## Insight
B2B truck buyers need to trust that precision steering can be relied upon over millions of kilometres. No spec sheet makes this claim viscerally credible. A demonstration that is physically impossible to fake does.

## Mechanic
Jean-Claude Van Damme performs a full lateral split between two reversing Volvo trucks at dawn, filmed in a single continuous take. The film demonstrates the trucks' Dynamic Steering system through an act of genuine physical extremity — not CGI, not editing.

## Why it worked
The demonstration is undeniable: Van Damme's body at full extension between two moving trucks cannot be faked in camera. The cinematic beauty of the execution (dawn, Enya, slow motion) transforms a product claim into a piece of film worth watching independently. Targeted at fleet buyers, it overdelivered to a global consumer audience.

## Steal
Find the product attribute that can be demonstrated in a way that would be physically impossible without the product's actual performance. Make the demonstration beautiful enough that it travels beyond the category audience.
