# The Proud Whopper (Burger King · 2014)

## Insight
The San Francisco Pride Parade draws hundreds of thousands of people. Burger King operates in the neighbourhood. What if the brand's support for Pride wasn't a campaign but a product — and a moment of personal discovery?

## Mechanic
During SF Pride 2014, Burger King sold a "Proud Whopper" — a rainbow-wrapped Whopper with a message inside the wrapper: "We are all the same inside." The burger was identical to a regular Whopper. Customers only discovered this when they opened the wrapper.

## Why it worked
The product IS the message — the reveal (same burger inside) enacts the campaign's truth in the customer's hands. P04 (Brand as Activist) through product action rather than statement. P09 (User as Co-Author): every customer who unwraps and reads is participating in the experiment. The PR is the product experience.

## Steal
When your brand wants to make a values statement, make it through the product rather than around it. The physical encounter with the product truth is always more credible than the surrounding campaign.
