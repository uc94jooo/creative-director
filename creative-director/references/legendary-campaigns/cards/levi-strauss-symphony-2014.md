# Symphony (Levi's · 2014)

## Insight

TODO: Year inferred; verify against campaign records.

Denim is the common fabric of American life across all social divides — the one garment that belongs to everyone regardless of class, background, or age.

## Mechanic

Long-form film using the metaphor of a symphony — different instruments (people, places, stories) coming together — to position Levi's denim as the connective fabric of American life.

## Why it worked

Long-form drama: the symphony metaphor gave the film structure while the diverse casting made the "belonging to everyone" claim visually credible.

## Steal

Musical metaphors (orchestra, harmony, chorus) work for brand films about community because they carry built-in emotional architecture. Use them when diversity of people is the message.
