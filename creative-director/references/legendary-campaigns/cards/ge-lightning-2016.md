# Lightning (GE · 2016)

## Insight
GE's power-generation technology is about harnessing elemental forces — electricity, turbines, energy at scale. Lightning is electricity at its most primal and spectacular. What if you took that metaphor literally?

## Mechanic
A film from GE's BBDO absurdist series, centred on lightning as a visual and conceptual metaphor — likely exploring electricity's raw power through a surreal extended scenario that connects natural electrical phenomena to GE's industrial harnessing of the same force.

## Why it worked
Absurdism (P11) allows GE to connect its industrial technology to something universally spectacular (lightning) without a technical explanation. The visual spectacle is the argument. Long-form (P16) gives the metaphor enough runtime to build from wonder to product connection.

## Steal
Natural phenomena (lightning, fire, water) are pre-existing spectacular visual assets. If your product works with or through those phenomena, using them as the creative premise costs nothing but stakes the product claim on the audience's existing awe.
