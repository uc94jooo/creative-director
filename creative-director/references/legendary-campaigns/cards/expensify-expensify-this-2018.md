# Expensify This (Expensify · 2018)

## Insight

Expense reporting is everyone's least favorite work task — even rockstars have to do it. Expensify is the brand that acknowledges the absurdity of the category.

## Mechanic

Music video with rapper 2 Chainz and Lil Dicky celebrating the glamour of expensing things, turning the mundane act of submitting receipts into an aspirational lifestyle statement via full hip-hop production.

## Why it worked

Absurdism as carrier: by taking the category's most boring feature (receipt submission) and staging it in the genre least associated with accounting, the contrast became the joke and the brand became memorable in a category where no one has a personality.

## Steal

Find the most boring function of your product. Ask what genre of culture could not be further from that function. Collide them completely. The gap is the humor.
