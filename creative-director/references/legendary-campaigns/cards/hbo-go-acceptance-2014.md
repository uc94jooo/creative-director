# Acceptance (HBO Go · 2014)

## Insight
When a family member who disapproves of your lifestyle choices watches HBO content with you that validates or depicts that lifestyle, the shared viewing becomes an involuntary disclosure and confrontation.

## Mechanic
A short film in the "Awkward Family Viewing" series exploring the layered discomfort when HBO content inadvertently surfaces personal truths in a family viewing situation — with HBO Go offered as the exit from communal screening.

## Why it worked
The series works as a campaign because each film targets a specific relationship configuration and discomfort type, building a taxonomy of awkward viewing that collectively covers a wide audience while each individual film feels precisely targeted.

## Steal
Design a campaign series where each execution isolates a single, specific instance of a broader insight. The series covers the full insight; each film earns its place by naming something the others don't.
