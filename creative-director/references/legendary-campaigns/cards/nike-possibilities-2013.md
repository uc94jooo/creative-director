# Possibilities (Nike · 2013)

## Insight
Athletic aspiration is most powerfully stated not as achievement already reached but as a bet placed against the odds — a wager that what seems impossible is only temporarily impossible. Nike's entire brand rests on the defiance of expected limits.

## Mechanic
A manifesto-style film narrated as a series of "what ifs" — proposing audacious sporting scenarios that defy conventional limits, from beating legends to recovering from injuries that should end careers. Each "what if" is stated with certainty rather than question.

## Why it worked
Framing possibility as a declaration rather than a wish changes the emotional register from hope to intention. The film positions Nike not as witness to athletes' success but as the voice that dares them to believe it before the world does. The long-form format earns emotional accumulation.

## Steal
When the brand stands for possibility, the advertising should not describe past possibilities but declare future ones. Prediction as brand voice, not aspiration as narrative.
