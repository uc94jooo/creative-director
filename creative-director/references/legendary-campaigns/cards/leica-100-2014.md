# 100 (Leica · 2014)

## Insight

Photography's power is not in the camera but in the moment it captures. The greatest photographs in history were possible because someone was there, with the right tool, at the exact right second.

## Mechanic

Film marking Leica's 100th anniversary by recreating iconic historical moments of the 20th century — showing what would have been lost if the photographer hadn't been there with a Leica. The camera is the hero because the moment is the hero.

## Why it worked

Craft as message meets long-form drama: a centenary film that made the camera's function inseparable from historical memory. By showing famous photographs being taken rather than the photographs themselves, Leica claimed credit for the moments, not just the images.

## Steal

For heritage brands, the product's history of use is often more powerful than its current capabilities. Make a film about what your product has been present for, not what it can do.
