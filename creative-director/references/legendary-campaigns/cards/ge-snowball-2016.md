# Snowball (GE · 2016)

## Insight
Technology's momentum, once started, builds on itself — innovations compounding like a snowball gaining mass as it rolls. GE's ecosystem of interconnected technologies is larger than any single product.

## Mechanic
A film in GE's absurdist campaign series, likely exploring the snowball metaphor — technology or consequence accumulating with surreal, unstoppable momentum. Part of the BBDO creative series that made GE technology culturally accessible through extended absurdist storytelling.

## Why it worked
Absurdism (P11) as metaphor: using a snowball's physical properties (growth, momentum, unstoppability) to communicate an abstract idea about technological compounding. Long-form format (P16) allows the metaphor to build to a satisfying, surreal conclusion.

## Steal
Physical metaphors (snowball, domino, chain) are inherently absurdist when taken to their logical visual extreme. Use them to communicate abstract brand concepts — compounding, momentum, inevitability — without asserting them in voiceover.
