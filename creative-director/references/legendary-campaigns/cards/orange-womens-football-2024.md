# WoMen's Football (Orange · 2024)

## Insight
Women's football is systematically undervalued — by audiences, media, and even fans who claim to support it. The film exposes this bias by exploiting it and then reversing it.

## Mechanic
A film that appears to show spectacular male football skills from the French national team. At the reveal, it is shown that the skills belong to the women's team — the men's footage was VFX overlay. The tagline: "Support the women's team as you support the men's." Won Cannes Grand Prix.

## Why it worked
Truth telling (P13): the reveal is not a twist for entertainment — it is proof of the audience's own bias. You watched the skills and assumed they belonged to men. Behavior inversion (P03): the film inverts the expectation and then names what you just did. The viewer is implicated; the message is undeniable.

## Steal
The most powerful argument for equality is the one the audience makes against themselves. Construct a situation where the audience's own assumption becomes the data. The reveal does not need to be angry — it just needs to be irrefutable.
