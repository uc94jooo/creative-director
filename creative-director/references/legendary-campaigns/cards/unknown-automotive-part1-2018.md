## Mechanic
A multi-part film series (Part 1 / Part 2) showcasing automotive performance through craft filmmaking — cinematography, sound design, and vehicle movement are primary communication vehicles.

## Why it worked
Product as proof (P14): performance claims are demonstrated visually rather than stated. Craft as message (P08): the production quality signals the product quality. The two-part format creates anticipation and extends earned attention across multiple releases.

## Steal
Split a performance story across two films to build anticipation. The craft of the film should mirror the craft of the product — a badly shot car film undermines its own claim of precision engineering.
