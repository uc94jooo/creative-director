# More GEICO Campaigns (GEICO · 2020)

## Insight
Insurance is the most avoided, least engaged-with category in advertising. GEICO's counter-thesis: if you make insurance advertising genuinely funny — not "insurance funny" but actually funny — people will watch voluntarily and share organically.

## Mechanic
A sustained multi-character, multi-format absurdist advertising platform across Gecko, Cavemen, Kash, Hump Day Camel, and numerous one-off executions. Each character owned distinct tonal territory while remaining under the GEICO brand umbrella. The platform's depth — 20+ years of irreverent execution — made any single GEICO ad a brand signal, not just an impression.

## Why it worked
No single GEICO character or campaign explains the brand's effectiveness — the accumulated signal of consistent, genuinely funny absurdism over decades does. The brand built tonal equity rather than claim equity. "GEICO" now means "unexpectedly funny insurance" regardless of which character appears.

## Steal
Tonal brand equity compounds. A single funny ad is an impression. Twenty years of consistent tonal commitment is a cultural position. Start early, commit fully, rotate characters and formats — but never rotate the tone.
