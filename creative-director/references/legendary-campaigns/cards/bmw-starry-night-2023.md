# Starry Night (BMW · 2023)

## Insight
BMW's electric vehicles represent a new relationship between technology and nature — performance without pollution, precision without aggression. "Starry Night" suggests a visual language drawn from Van Gogh's most iconic painting: swirling energy, luminous motion, the beauty of the night sky.

## Mechanic
A campaign combining technology (P18) — likely projection, digital art, or generative AI — with participatory or co-creation elements (P09), using "Starry Night" visual language to communicate BMW's electric vehicle promise through artistic expression rather than technical specification.

## Why it worked
Automotive brands face the challenge of communicating electric vehicle emotion in a category built on combustion metaphors. Art — specifically post-Impressionist movement and light — offers a visual language for energy that isn't built on exhaust or speed. P18 and P09 together create an experience that audiences can share.

## Steal
When your brand is crossing from one technology era to another, use art that already communicates the new territory's emotions. Van Gogh's swirling energy pre-coded the feeling of electric motion before the product existed.
