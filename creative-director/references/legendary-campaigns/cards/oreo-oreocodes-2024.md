# OreoCodes (Oreo · 2024)

## Insight
Every Oreo cookie has a unique embossed pattern — a physical detail that billions of people have touched but never interpreted as a code.

## Mechanic
Oreo turned the cookie's surface pattern into scannable codes that unlocked rewards, content, and collectible experiences, making the physical product an interactive digital interface.

## Why it worked
The product itself became the medium. Users had an existing object that suddenly had new utility — zero barrier to entry, since everyone already has an Oreo. The mechanic rewarded attention to something previously unnoticed.

## Steal
Find the overlooked physical detail of your product and make it load-bearing. Existing artifacts become participation mechanics when you give them new interpretive weight.
