# Major (Hennessy V.S. · 2016)

## Insight
Hennessy's heritage in Black culture and its genuine role in celebrations of achievement — from personal milestones to community victories — is an authentic cultural truth the brand could anchor. "We Do What We Must" positions the brand as companion to genuine striving.

## Mechanic
Long-form film featuring real achievers — likely including music, sport, or cultural figures — whose stories of discipline and triumph are intercut with Hennessy as the drink of earned celebration. The serialization element (P10) suggests this is part of an ongoing brand narrative.

## Why it worked
Long-form drama (P16): achievement stories require extended runtime to convey their emotional weight. Serialization and ritual (P10): the recurring format builds a brand library of achievement stories over time, each adding to the cumulative brand truth. The product earns its place as the drink of genuine achievement rather than aspirational fantasy.

## Steal
For prestige spirits, genuine cultural authenticity — not borrowed credibility — is the only defensible positioning. Build a library of real stories over time rather than a single hero film.
