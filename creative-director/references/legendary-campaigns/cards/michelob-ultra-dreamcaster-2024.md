# Dreamcaster (Michelob Ultra · 2024)

## Insight

Blind sports fans cannot experience basketball the way sighted fans do — play-by-play commentary doesn't capture the spatial relationships, body language, and visual drama that make the game great. They deserve better.

## Mechanic

Michelob Ultra trained and deployed AI that provided spatial audio commentary for visually impaired NBA fans — translating the court's visual geometry into 3D audio that let blind fans experience the spatial reality of play for the first time.

## Why it worked

Tech as canvas meets truth telling: a genuine accessibility gap was addressed with technology that delivered a genuinely better experience — not an accommodation, but an improvement. Michelob Ultra's "championing of sport enjoyment" brand platform was demonstrated through action, not claimed through words.

## Steal

Find the audience your category has structurally excluded and use available technology to serve them with something genuinely better than what everyone else gets. The story of inclusion earns media; the product earns loyalty.
